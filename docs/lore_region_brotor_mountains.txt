# Brotor Mountains

## Type
- Region (mountain range)
- Part of: eastern Crevalon, bordering the Slaerian Desert

## Description
- The Brotor Mountains form the eastern border of Crevalon, rising between the kingdom's heartland and the Slaerian Desert beyond.
- The mountains contain abandoned villages that have been overtaken by Shadows (undead creatures).
- The Signet Monks have a monastery or retreat somewhere in the mountains.

## Key Locations & Events
- Abandoned village — the earlier era party discovered a village here that had been overtaken by Shadows; all inhabitants were gone.
- Signet Monks' location — the party visited the Signet Monks here and met Zana, a monk who was nearly killed by the Aviary during their visit. Zana entrusted the black gem she had been guarding — and an orb of dragonkind — to the party before they departed.

## Connections
- Eastern border of Crevalon
- Borders the Slaerian Desert to the east
- The Slaerian Desert lies beyond the mountains on the way toward Ikovia
