# Vormark (The Skyforge City)

## Type
- City (hangar city / airship hub)
- Part of: Anearios (western region of Windenreich)

## Description
- Vormark is a bustling hangar city built atop the cliffs that overlook the western reaches of Windenreich in Anearios.
- From sunrise to dusk, the skyline hums with the rhythmic thunder of airship engines and the glint of brass hulls.
- The air always smells faintly of ozone, salt, and hot metal. Steam vents hiss in every street; banners of skyship guilds ripple in the wind.
- Music and engine noise blur together; mechanics hammer hull plates while street performers play brass instruments.
- The city never truly sleeps; there is always a ship taking off or landing, and lights from the docks shimmer like constellations at night.
- Tagline: "Where the wind is never still and every coin rides the clouds."

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The Aether Docks — giant elevated hangars and shipyards where airships are constructed, refueled, and repaired; dock workers swarm the decks; smugglers operate from the lower gantries away from customs patrols; Dock 17 is specifically referenced in Campaign 3 (Kira is tracking a shipment manifest)
- Windspire Row — a vertical district of stacked housing and taverns built into the cliffs; a favorite stop for skyfarers and adventurers; raucous and alive with stories of storms, monsters, and lost aether-veins in the west
- The Skyforge — the original foundry where Vormark began; still active; manufactures propulsion cores and stabilizing rings for airships; apprentices and artificers work side-by-side amid sparks and roaring furnaces
- Guild Hall of the Wayfarers' Concord — governs airship travel, trade tariffs, and flight rights; bureaucratic and self-important; their seal is needed for any major expedition westward
- The Lower Gantries — beneath the main docks; where old ships are cannibalized for parts; dangerous; full of thieves, black-market tech dealers, and unofficial repair crews

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- The Clockwork Hands — use Vormark's Docks (specifically the Iron Assembly front organization) to move experimental propulsion cores to Giglington; Vormark is their staging ground for new machine development
- Yutei (Campaign 3) — ice sorcerer; arrived in Vormark after fleeing Evlinross; took temporary work at the Aether Docks helping regulate core temperatures; possibly used ice magic as a street performer
- Kira Cavrick (Campaign 3) — came to Vormark following a shipment manifest trail linked to Ren Dovair's murder; investigating Dock 17
- Yasmine (Campaign 3) — arrived in Vormark to find/protect Kira; has her own reasons related to forbidden relics trafficking
- Nymira (Campaign 3) — woke up in Vormark; was found drifting in on a battered cargo skiff from the western mists; the dockworkers assumed she was a crash victim from the Western Front

## History & Lore
- Vormark grew around the Aether Docks; the city serves as both a trade nexus and an industrial frontier.
- The Campaign 3 party all converges in Vormark before boarding the Vox Eterna to Giglington: an explosion at the Aether Docks (involving Kira's investigation of Dock 17) brings the party together; they discover the Clockwork Hands' emblem stamped on a shattered propulsion core.
- Strange storms have closed western routes from Vormark; the Stormfront Contracts hire brave crews to chart new paths.
- A mysterious explosion destroyed one of the Aether Cradles before Campaign 3 begins; witnesses saw a cloaked figure with a mechanical arm flee the scene.
- The Missing Vessel Hollow Reign: vanished on its maiden voyage; hull fragments drift back humming with strange energy.

## Connections
- Western Windenreich region of Anearios
- The Vox Eterna (Crown Line flagship) departs from Vormark bound for Giglington (the capital)
- The Aether Rail eventually connects Giglington to Tiven's Read; Vormark is the western departure/arrival hub for airships
- Smugglers moving illegal tech from Vormark's lower gantries to Giglington are connected to the Clockwork Hands

## Important Notes
- Vormark is in the WESTERN region of ANEARIOS — NOT in Crevalon or Ikovia.
- Vormark is an AIRSHIP HUB and INDUSTRIAL FRONTIER city — NOT the capital of Anearios (Giglington is the capital).
- The Aether Docks in Vormark are a CLOCKWORK HANDS staging ground during Campaign 3 — the docks are dangerous for the party once their investigation is known.
- Direct spellcasting is BANNED in Anearios including Vormark; magical use must be imbued within machines or objects.
