# Sharnwick

## Type
- Village (small village)
- Part of: Crevalon (rural, central region within the Grudoulan Valley)

## Description
- Sharnwick is a small village in Crevalon known primarily for sheep farming and fine wool production.
- A modest, rural settlement; primarily human inhabitants in modest attire.
- The village has a main square with a makeshift stage where public announcements are made.
- Fine wools produced here are taken via merchant cart to Aberdeen to be sold.
- The village worships Chauntea (goddess of agriculture), as do many surrounding areas.
- Virion Maganti's father was a farmer from Sharnwick who was killed by bandits while going town to town to sell goods.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The Sharnwick Pint — the local tavern; owned and run by Beth (a short, stocky woman with a cowbell who keeps order); serves mutton stew as the specialty dish; 5 silver per room per night, 3 silver per meal
- Sam's General Store — run by Sam; sells farming tools; stocks a map of the kingdom; the party stole a map here
- A storage shed outside the general store — where wool is stored before transport to Aberdeen
- The fortune teller's hut — on the edge of town; a makeshift, run-down hut belonging to Ange; she has lived here since her family's tragedy decades ago
- A blacksmith — Jackson; does basic horseshoes; directs customers to Elmshire for more complex work
- A vegetable market — old ladies gossip and watch strangers
- The village stable — where Geoffrey was kept prisoner briefly after his arrest

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Beth — tavern owner/barkeep of The Sharnwick Pint; short, stocky; uses a cowbell to restore order; apologizes for the village idiots (John, Sam, and Todd)
- Ange — the fortune teller; lives on the outskirts of town; a warlock with a Pact of the Fiend (made a deal with Silvester, a servant of Bel of Avernus, after losing her family in a fire); she has blue wise-looking eyes; wears a weathered purple dress; the village folk visit her out of pity while pretending to want fortunes read; she eventually disappears after her role in the story
- Tommy Breckenridge — old raisin-faced man who drinks at the bar; suspects the fortune teller in the sheep disappearances
- Geoffrey — a merchant's son from Sharnwick; was given a Solarian talisman in Aberdeen by monks and was compelled through dreams to make sheep disappear as an experiment; not malicious; was imprisoned briefly in the horse stable
- Hugh — Geoffrey's father; a merchant; regrets taking Geoffrey to Aberdeen; gives the party his cart in apology
- John, Sam, and Todd — three strapping young farmhands; the "village idiots"; initially antagonize the party; later frightened away by the supernatural sheep event
- Jim — a trainee guard who frequently gets drunk at The Sharnwick Pint; passes out in the corner; described as "useless" by Beth
- Martha, Barbara, Anne — old ladies at the vegetable market; gossip and stare at strangers
- Mathy — a man who keeps a horse farm; can supply horses if the party needs them

## History & Lore
- Sharnwick is where Campaign 1 begins; the party gathers to investigate the mysterious disappearance of sheep.
- The sheep disappearances were caused by Geoffrey, who was compelled by a Solarian sun talisman to conduct sacrifices ("to make the wolf-headed jackals appear" and "to have villages worship the sun god").
- Ange made her pact with the fiend Silvester (a pit fiend serving Bel of Avernus) after losing her husband and son in a tragic fire. She was manipulated and has suffered social isolation as a result; the village views her with pity and fear.
- Ange gave the Solarian sun talisman (which came into her possession 3 months earlier in Aberdeen) to the party in an infernal puzzle box to contain it.
- Virion Maganti's father was a farmer from Sharnwick; bandits attacked and killed him on a trade route. No one was ever brought to justice; this injustice drove Virion to necromancy in an attempt to raise his father.

## Connections
- Located in the Grudoulan Valley, central Crevalon
- On the road between the interior and Elmshire (city between Sharnwick and Aberdeen)
- Sharnwick → Elmshire → Aberdeen is the main road route toward the coast
- Ange can scry on the party after they leave; she later disappears (possibly dimension-doored away)
- Virion's origin story connects Sharnwick to the main Campaign 1 villain

## Important Notes
- Sharnwick is a SMALL SHEEP-FARMING VILLAGE — NOT a city, NOT a port, NOT a military town.
- The fortune teller Ange is NOT evil by nature; she made a desperate deal and has been manipulated; she tried to help the party by giving them the infernal puzzle box.
- The sheep sacrifices were an EXPERIMENT by Solarian agents — testing whether local communities could be converted to Solarian worship through supernatural intimidation. Geoffrey was an unwitting instrument.
- The infernal puzzle box Ange gave the party is a high-DC magic item (DC 30 Intelligence Investigation to open safely; failure risks 12d6 psychic damage). It was made from materials from the Nine Hells.
