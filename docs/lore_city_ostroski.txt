# Ostroski

## Type
- City
- Coastal city in Ikovia
- Part of: Ikovia (eastern continent)

## Description
- Ostroski is a coastal city in Ikovia; its ships are styled like Viking longships
- One of the major cities of the Ikovian Empire
- During the events of more recently: the party traveled here; death clerics in Ostroski were used to resurrect Queen Katerina Baranovsky after she was killed

## Notable Inhabitants / Events
- Site of Queen Katerina's resurrection — the party brought death clerics here to resurrect the Ikovian Queen after she was assassinated

## Important Notes
- Ostroski is a CITY in Ikovia, NOT a person or character — never describe Ostroski as a necromancer or any kind of individual
- It is a coastal city known for its Viking-style ships and for being the location where Queen Katerina was resurrected
