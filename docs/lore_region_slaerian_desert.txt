# The Slaerian Desert

## Type
- Region (desert)
- Part of: Crevalon (eastern border), transitioning into Ikovia

## Description
- The Slaerian Desert is a vast desert region to the east of the Brotor Mountains; it forms the eastern border of Crevalon and the western approach to the Ikovian Empire.
- Created during the DragonMage War when the red dragon serving the Solarian King set many forests ablaze, forming the Slaerian Desert permanently.
- The landscape includes a desert expanse with a canyon range further east.
- Beyond the desert (far east) lies another mountain range, then the Ikovian Empire.
- There is a fighter Queen (separate kingdom) who has a low opinion of King Titus, located beyond the desert mountain range with dwarven clans in the mountains.
- Dwarves hold mines near the other side of the desert mountain range; a dwarven clan occupies the mountains further north.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- A canyon range in the far east of the desert
- The mountain range beyond the desert that borders Ikovia from the desert
- Solarian temple — a Solarian stronghold located within or at the edge of the desert; surrounded by a force field; contains dragon artifacts; the Campaign 1 party needs a spell scroll from the Sylvanian Academy to dispel the force field
- Occupied villages — the Paladins of Siadiff push east across the Slaerian Desert during Campaign 2, capturing and occupying Ikovian villages near the desert

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- The fighter Queen — rules a separate kingdom beyond the desert mountain range; has low opinion of King Titus; not aligned with Crevalon
- Dwarven clans — occupy mines and mountains at the desert's far edges
- Ikovian refugees — during Campaign 2, Crevalon refugees flee through this region; Ikovian forces assist them

## History & Lore
- Origin: The Slaerian Desert was formed during the DragonMage War thousands of years ago when the red dragon (serving the Solarian King) set forests ablaze. The conflagration permanently burned the region into desert.
- The Solarian monks use desert routes to travel between their base/temple and Crevalon's western cities. Their temple is somewhere in or near the desert.
- During Campaign 2: The Paladins of Siadiff launch their holy crusade east, crossing the Slaerian Desert to reach Ikovian territory. Villages near the desert border become flashpoints of conflict; Brezbin is one such occupied village.

## Connections
- Eastern border of Crevalon, separated from the Grudoulan Valley by the Brotor Mountains
- Transitional zone between Crevalon and Ikovia
- The Solarian temple is somewhere in this region
- Brezbin (Ikovia, southern region) is on the far eastern side of the desert/mountain border
- Campaign 1: Scirocco is from the desert; her origins are tied to the Slaerian Desert (her mother Khadja is connected to a mage in the desert — Virion Maganti)

## Important Notes
- The Slaerian Desert was created by the RED DRAGON — NOT the white dragon (which created the FrostLands).
- The Brotor Mountains separate the Slaerian Desert from the Grudoulan Valley / main Crevalon territory.
- The Solarian temple in/near the desert requires a special spell scroll (obtained from the Sylvanian Academy in Handletare) to enter — it is protected by a force field.
- The desert is NOT in Ikovia; it is the TRANSITION ZONE between Crevalon and Ikovia.
