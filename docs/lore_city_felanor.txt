# Felanor

## Type
- City (elven city, "The Green City")
- Part of: Crevalon (northern region, roughly in the area between Handletare and Finreld)

## Description
- Felanor is an elven city known as "The Green City"; its older buildings were originally made from copper that has since turned to a beautiful light green patina over time.
- The city is surrounded by orchards with beautiful green apple trees; the countryside rolls with hills; the overall aesthetic is verdant, elegant, and refined.
- Located "not too far outside" the city is Lady Vesemir's estate — a grand four-story elven-style manor with gilded embellishments, a circular entrance drive, a central fountain with marble nymph figures, and beautifully maintained grounds.
- Felanor is in many ways similar to Finreld but described as "everything was green, including their white chocolate" — a detail shared by a character who visited Diondria and Aberdeen and mentioned Felanor as a notable northern city.
- The city is close to Handletare and within the northern region of Crevalon; letters to Ari reference "a few weeks' time, close to your home" for a Felanor contest, implying proximity to Handletare.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- Lady Vesemir's Manor — an elegant four-story elven manor on a rolling hill outside the city proper; wide horizontal facade; gilded embellishments; a fountain at the circular entrance; venue for Lady Vesemir's annual masquerade ball; "quite a beautiful, well-kept manor not too far outside the city of Felanor itself" (Florian's description)
- The city itself — elven architecture in green-patinated copper; orchards; fine cultural offerings

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Lady Vesemir (full formal title: Lady Almithara Vesemir) — the middle-aged daughter of the late Lord and Lady Vesemir; she has not remarried; she hosts an annual masquerade ball that draws Crevalon aristocracy; she is a famous ballerina; she is eventually revealed to be Caeda's mother (a fling covered up); descended from Daereis Vesemir (also called "The Third Daughter of Corellon"), an ancient elven mage who vanquished the green dragon 4,000 years ago and imprisoned it in a gem necklace; Lady Vesemir wears this gem necklace at her masquerade events
- Daereis Vesemir — ancient elven ancestor of Lady Vesemir; depicted in texts as a tall, slender figure casting an impressive spell; she imprisoned the ancient green dragon in the gem necklace worn by her descendant
- Sir Kelvhan Umeric — Attendant to the House of Vesemir; sends invitations to parties on Lady Vesemir's behalf
- Anton — a traveler connected to the Babushka (the earlier era); was in Finreld then traveled to Felanor with a league; sent a letter from Felanor

## History & Lore
- Felanor is one of the elven noble cities of Crevalon; Lady Vesemir's family has hosted annual masquerade balls and artistic competitions for years.
- 4,000 years ago, Daereis Vesemir imprisoned the ancient green dragon in a gem that became an heirloom necklace. This necklace has been passed down through the Vesemir family and Lady Vesemir wears it publicly at her balls.
- During the earlier era: Florian Reiter discovers from historical texts that the necklace Lady Vesemir wears at her masquerade ball is almost certainly the dragon-prison artifact — the Aviary's next target. The party attends the masquerade in disguise to protect Lady Vesemir.
- The night of the masquerade: an Aviary operative attempts to steal the necklace from Lady Vesemir; the party intervenes. Lady Vesemir is compromised during the encounter but ultimately survives. The party secures the artifact.
- Gathadriel Vilistra — a poet met at the masquerade.
- Caeda's parentage: Lady Vesemir is confirmed as Caeda's mother; the connection is a long-kept secret that Florian noticed (remarking that Caeda looks strikingly similar to the Vesemirs).

## Connections
- In the northern region of Crevalon, near Handletare; described as "close to your home" in a letter to Ari who studies at Handletare
- Finreld is within traveling distance (letters mention a contest in Finreld, then a follow-up contest in Felanor)
- Adrastea Orvir (Handletare) knew that the Lady of Felanor (Lady Vesemir) was protecting one of the imprisoned dragons in her necklace
- Florian Reiter acts as escort/protector for the party at Lady Vesemir's event; he knows Felanor's noble social scene

## Important Notes
- Felanor is a NOBLE ELVEN CITY known for culture, orchards, and aristocratic events — NOT a magical academy city, NOT a paladin city.
- Lady Vesemir of Felanor holds a GREEN DRAGON imprisoned in her necklace gem — not the same as any other dragon artifact.
- Felanor is distinct from Finreld (which is a port city) and from Handletare (which has the Sylvanian Academy). Felanor is primarily known for its noble house.
- Lady Vesemir is revealed to be Caeda's mother — a key the earlier era plot revelation.
- The Vesemir family heirloom necklace is both a family artifact and a dragon prison.
