# Caeda

## Identity
- Cleric of Rao (god of reason, discourse, and peace)
- Race: Elf (noted as looking "strikingly similar to the Vesemirs"; Florian commented on this resemblance)
- Possible connection to the Vesemir family of Felanor (Florian asked her directly: "Have you any relation to the Vesimir family in Felanor?"; they spoke of her origins; there is a DM note that "Lady Vesemir could be her mom — she had a fling that was covered up")
- Player character in Campaign 1

## Role & Affiliation
- Elven cleric of Rao; joined the Campaign 1 group at the King's request
- Rao is described as the god of reason, discourse, and peace; his clerics pursue knowledge, logic, and peaceful means but are not above force
- Based primarily in Crevalon during Campaign 1; later in Campaign 2 she is captured by Siadiff paladins (30 years after Campaign 1)

## History & Origin
- Background details are partially intentionally withheld (DM notes reference her origins being discussed with Florian over a picnic); the Vesemir connection is implied but not confirmed in-text
- Was eventually captured by paladins (Knights of Torm / Siadiff forces) in Campaign 2; her daughter Rose Reiter and Florian went to find her together; they fought through an underground cave network near the mountain ridge that borders Ikovia from the desert; discovered she had been moved; her whereabouts remained a mystery beyond that cave location

## Abilities & Powers
- Cleric of Rao: healing, resurrection, and holy spells
- Led Elly in peaceful meditation and reflection; presided over Elly's vows at the Temple of Rao in Finreld
- Persuaded the priestesses at the Temple of Rao to open their eyes to lessening certain restrictions
- Performed a resurrection ritual (with Florian's assistance) during Campaign 1

## Key Relationships
- Florian Reiter: fell in love with him over the course of Campaign 1; Florian confessed his love on a castle balcony and she confirmed she felt the same; they went on a lunar picnic, waltzed under the moonlit sky, and flew together (he carried her on his wings)
- Elly: fellow party member; close enough that Florian worried about Caeda's safety
- The party (Elly, Scirocco, Ari, Babushka, Pavel): core Campaign 1 companions
- Lady Vesemir: possible family connection (unconfirmed); Florian commented on the resemblance
- Rose Reiter: Caeda is Rose's mother (confirmed in Campaign 2 — "those captured was your mother, Caeda"; "her father, Florian, was waiting for her")

## Key Events (campaign)
- Campaign 1: Joined the party at the King's request; traveled with them through Crevalon, Finreld, the desert, and the Elantir Forest
- Was present for and participated in a resurrection ritual with Florian
- Received a confession of love from Florian on a castle balcony; accepted and reciprocated
- Went on a moonlit picnic with Florian in the lavender fields outside Diondria; he read poetry and they waltzed
- Flew with Florian over the city on his wings
- Attended Lady Vesemir's masquerade ball
- Campaign 2 (30 years later): Was captured by Siadiff paladins; Rose and Florian went to find her, discovered she was moved from the initial cave location; her fate beyond that point was still being investigated as the campaign unfolded

## Important Notes
- Caeda is a player character, not an NPC, but she has significant lore ties through her relationship with Florian and her possible Vesemir heritage
- The Vesemir connection is DM-notes level — it is "Lady Vesemir could be her mom — she had a fling that was covered up" — it has NOT been confirmed in-game at this point
- She is the mother of Rose Reiter (a Campaign 2 player character), making Rose also the daughter of Florian Reiter
- Rose is described as having "Father, Florian, was not with her at the time and subsequently not captured"
