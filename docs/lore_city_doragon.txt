# Doragon

## Type
- City (dwarven mountain city)
- Part of: the Brotor Mountains / eastern Crevalon

## Description
- Doragon is a dwarven city carved directly into the face of a massive mountain.
- Its structures are impressive architectural feats, gilded with intricate metal designs that glint in the sunlight along the mountainside.
- The sounds of mining picks clanging can be heard on approach, and showers of orange sparks rise from the many anvils tended by dwarven blacksmiths throughout the city.
- The dwarves of Doragon are connected to Madame Alora Venyette — they assisted her after she performed the dragon-sealing ritual during the DragonMage War, reviving her with smelling salts and providing animal blood.

## Connections
- Located in or near the Brotor Mountains
- The dwarves of Doragon are allied with Alora Venyette; this connection is to Alora, NOT to Lady Orvir
