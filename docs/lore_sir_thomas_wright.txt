# Sir Thomas Wright

## Identity
- Nature/type: Paladin (possessed by a demon)
- Race/species: Human (implied)
- Full name: Sir Thomas Wright
- The demon possessing him: Fraz-Urb'luu, Prince of Deception (176th layer of the Abyss, Hollow Heart)

## Role & Affiliation
- Leader of the Paladins of Siadiff (the holy city in Crevalon's southern region)
- Appointed King of the Crevalon Kingdom after King Titus died with no heir (30 years after Campaign 1)
- Head of the paladin forces invading Ikovia in Campaign 2
- Connected to Siadiff (the Order of the Golden Lion's holy city)
- Campaign 1 (minor appearance) and Campaign 2 (major villain)

## History & Origin
- During Campaign 1, was a ranking paladin within the Order of the Golden Lion at Siadiff; had an audience with Alora at the King's request but was dismissive and hostile toward her
- Alora detected a "dual nature, a darker nature — a shadow over his countenance, a glint of red within his eye" during that meeting, suggesting possession was already underway or beginning
- The possession by Fraz-Urb'luu was triggered by the Demonomicon of Iggwilv — the book that Scirocco lost during Campaign 1 — which allowed the demon to possess Sir Thomas Wright
- Sir Thomas genuinely believes he has received a vision from Torm; he does not know the true nature of the Staff of Retribution and believes it is a holy relic that will help him "cleanse the world"
- When King Titus died (no heir), Sir Thomas — as head of the paladins — was appointed ruler of Crevalon, then used that authority to expand paladin control and launch war against Ikovia

## Abilities & Powers
- Paladin: armored, carries a sword; emblazoned with the glowing fist of Torm
- Under demonic possession: acts as a puppet of Fraz-Urb'luu while believing himself to be divinely guided
- Commands a large military force of Paladins of Siadiff

## Key Relationships
- Fraz-Urb'luu: the demon possessing him; manipulating him to gather the five pieces of the Staff of Fraz-Urb'luu (also called the Staff of Retribution in paladin propaganda)
- Sir Godwin: his "right hand" within the paladin order (as referenced by Alora in Campaign 2)
- Alora Venyette: had a hostile audience with her during Campaign 1; she sensed the demonic shadow over him
- King Titus: the previous king whose death left the throne vacant, leading to Thomas's appointment
- The Ikovian Empire: his forces are at war with Ikovia, ostensibly on a crusade but actually serving Fraz-Urb'luu's agenda

## Key Events
- Campaign 1: Met with Alora in the throne room on the King's behalf; showed contempt toward her as a vampire; Alora detected something wrong with him
- Campaign 2: Appointed King of Crevalon 30 years after Campaign 1; immediately began consolidating paladin power and imprisoning undesirables
- His troops began collecting the five pieces of the Staff of Retribution across Ikovia, framing the quest as recovering "a holy relic"
- The Ikovian Queen commissioned the Campaign 2 party (via Milly) to gather the staff pieces before Wright's forces could assemble it
- His occupation of Brezbin (Campaign 2 starting village) and the surrounding region is the inciting conflict of Campaign 2

## Important Notes
- Sir Thomas is a victim of demonic possession as much as he is a villain; he sincerely believes in his divine mission
- Fraz-Urb'luu is the actual intelligence directing him: Prince of Deception, ruler of the 176th layer of the Abyss called Hollow Heart
- The Demonomicon of Iggwilv (lost by Scirocco in Campaign 1) is the direct cause of the possession
- Do not conflate Sir Thomas Wright with Sir Godwin — Sir Godwin was a Campaign 1 paladin antagonist; Sir Thomas is the Campaign 2 villain; Thomas is described as "Sir Godwin's right hand" in Alora's account (meaning Thomas served under Godwin's order in Campaign 1)
- The Staff of Retribution is the same as the Staff of Fraz-Urb'luu: a weapon combining a staff of command, rod of beguiling, and rod of rulership, broken into 5 pieces
