# Lady Kuyomi

## Identity
- Fallen angel (solar-class celestial); fallen from grace and now inhabiting the Material Plane
- Also referred to as: "The Lunar Queen," "Lady Kuyomi," "the Lunarian queen"
- Race: Fallen angel / solar; described as having black wings (typically hidden), silver hair, glowing pupil-less eyes, and the power of a solar or greater
- The Lunarians (moon elves in the Elantir Forest) believe her to be a Material incarnation of the goddess Selune; they call themselves her "chosen"
- She is bisexual (confirmed in DM notes: "Kuyomi is bi")
- She is NOT fond of the Solarian King / sun god

## Role & Affiliation
- Queen of the Lunarians: leads the moon elves (lunar elves) who dwell in the Elantir Forest
- Commands silver, blue, green, black, and white dragons (her faction from the ancient DragonMage War, 4,000 years prior)
- Her settlement is in the Elantir Forest; floating towers that do not touch the ground are where she resides; the city is encased in a dome of perpetual moonlit night that she and her elders have magically maintained
- Her power and her settlement's power are tied to the moon; when the moonlight is blocked or weakened, they must ration power
- Sapphire (the silver dragon) is her emissary and later her love interest

## History & Origin
- Fell from grace thousands of years ago alongside the Solarian King; they were originally two angelic beings who fell in love with each other; their respective gods considered it an abandonment of duty (they neglected their protective duties, allowing a devil to kill a fellow angel or allowing mortal souls to be claimed by devils)
- After the fall, they resented each other and went to war, leading to the DragonMage War
- The mages eventually sealed away their dragon fleets, forcing a truce; they agreed to maintain balance (day and night in equal measure) while working to earn their way back into the heavens
- The Solarian King had not fully gotten over his love for Kuyomi, but she became disillusioned with him after his followers began aggressive incursions ("the acts committed against her kingdom were enough for her not to like him")
- Was supposed to marry the Solarian King (told so by her god, elders, or a dream), but by previously she has rejected this; eventually falls in love with Sapphire instead

## Abilities & Powers
- Solar-level or greater power; stat block described as "stats of a solar but stronger — enough to command Dragons"; CR approximately 30 (max damage, raised AC and HP)
- Two samurai swords that crackle with radiant energy in combat
- Has made the area around her settlement into a pocket dimension of perpetual magical night
- Commands all of the Lunarian faction's dragons (silver, blue, green, black, white)
- Has guards, assassins with crescent moon shurikens, and Pegasus-like silver horses with crescent moons on their foreheads

## Key Relationships
- Sapphire: her honor-bound silver dragon emissary; eventually becomes her love interest ("falls in love with Sapphire instead" of the Solarian King)
- The Solarian King: former love, current adversary; they fell from grace together; she now views him as a bigot given his followers' aggression; "The only one strong enough to fight him is me"
- Ellyjobell (Elly): Elly was told by the Lunarian court that she was the one to "squash the Solarians who are hurting her lover (the moon queen Kuyomi)" — Tatiana (Elly's fey patron, actually the Queen of Air and Darkness posing as Tatania) directed Elly toward this goal
- Virion Maganti: he sent the Bluejay to offer the Aviary's assistance in awakening Kuyomi's dragons; Kuyomi agreed to cooperate with this arrangement, which means Virion was manipulating her for his deal with Bel
- King Titus: implied romantic interest from the King's side (DM note: "King Titus is in love with the Lunarian queen as he can just visit the vampires to get to her")

## Key Events (campaign)
- previously: The party first reached the Lunarian settlement and appeared before her court; they heard "In the name of Our Lady Kuyomi, let us begin"
- Later the party met her directly in her throne room / floating tower; she informed them about the ancient war thousands of years ago and the nature of the fallen celestials
- After the red dragon was killed by Florian, she refused to send Sapphire on a "suicide mission": "I will not send you on a suicide mission. We will wait until we have awakened your brethren"
- Virion (via the Bluejay) offered the Aviary's assistance in awakening dragons; Kuyomi agreed; this was a manipulation by Virion

## Important Notes
- Kuyomi is NOT fond of the sun god / Solarian King, despite being told she was supposed to marry him; by previously she is in love with Sapphire
- The Lunarians believe she is a Material incarnation of Selune, the moon goddess
- Only those born under a full moon night may see her (access restriction to her chambers)
- Her power wanes as the moon wanes; something has been blocking moonlight to her settlement during previously, forcing them to ration power
- She and the Solarian King are equally matched in power; the only way to stop the conflict is to reconcile them (the party must "play matchmaker") OR have them confront each other directly
- If the Solarian King kills Kuyomi, the sky is thrown out of balance and the gods are angered, and the devil claims the Solarian's soul
