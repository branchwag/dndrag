# Duchess Jamila

## Identity
- Nature/type: Djinn (noble/duchess)
- Race/species: Djinn
- Full name: Duchess Jamila (no surname given in text)
- Relation: Khadja's mother; Scirocco's grandmother

## Role & Affiliation
- Duchess of the City of Avis on the Plane of Air
- Descended from the Wind Dukes of Aaqa (ancient djinn lineage dedicated to law and good, vigilant against elemental evil)
- Campaign 1

## History & Origin
- When Khadja disappeared (imprisoned in the ring by Virion), Jamila found only a ring left behind; she had the ring examined and it was enchanted — she kept it but could not break the spell
- Lived in a grand estate in the City of Avis; kept winged cats (celestially-winged feline companions: Parsnip, Ruby, and Sasha) and was attended by a butler named Yaccar Alami (an aarakocra)
- Her estate held a stable on the outskirts of Avis where she bred and raced pegasi (gradient pink-to-other winged horses)

## Abilities & Powers
- As a powerful djinn descended from the Wind Dukes of Aaqa, she has substantial magical ability
- Can cast Wish (unlike Khadja, who cannot cast Wish herself — the party was directed to Jamila or a grandfather to grant wishes on Khadja's behalf)
- Likely possesses the full range of djinn powers

## Key Relationships
- Khadja: her daughter; was overjoyed upon Khadja's return; had preserved the ring and been searching for her
- Scirocco: her granddaughter; the party brought Scirocco to meet her on the Plane of Air
- Lady Orvir: worked with Lady Orvir during the Plane of Air visit; was present when Lady Orvir freed her own imprisoned mother (Gwentharidel) using powerful magic
- Yaccar Alami: her aarakocra butler ("Khadja! Is that really you? The Duchess will be overjoyed — come in!")

## Key Events (Campaign 1)
- The party traveled to the Plane of Air with Khadja and Lady Orvir (Lady Orvir used an attuned rod to transport them and provided a magic carpet to reach the City of Avis)
- Reunion: Khadja led the party to Jamila's home; Jamila was overjoyed to see her daughter
- Jamila showed the party the ring she had found upon Khadja's disappearance; Lady Orvir examined it and found it enchanted identically to the ring that had held Khadja
- Lady Orvir broke the spell on Jamila's ring, freeing Jamila's own imprisoned mother, Gwentharidel (Gwendy appeared to have feebleminded symptoms afterward, later cured)
- The group went to the Griffon's Beak tavern together; the party played Drunken Djinn; an evening of drinks and dancing followed
- Lady Orvir theorized during the visit that Virion may have stored a piece of his soul in Avernus

## Important Notes
- Plays with a French accent in-game (lower register)
- Quote on difficult matters: "There are things beyond what the great winds have taught us"
- Her three winged cats — Parsnip, Ruby, and Sasha — serve as distinctive set dressing for her home
- "Descended from the Wind Dukes of Aaqa" — the Wind Dukes are dedicated to law and good and maintain a vigilant watch against elemental evil; this gives Jamila a lawful good alignment and a heritage of cosmic significance
- Do not confuse Jamila's situation (free djinn, living in the City of Avis) with Khadja's (imprisoned in a ring for decades)
- Do not confuse her relationship to the ring she found with Khadja's imprisonment ring — the ring Jamila found was enchanted identically but held a different person (later revealed to be Gwentharidel, Lady Orvir's mother)
