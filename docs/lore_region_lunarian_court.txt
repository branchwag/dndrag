# The Lunarian Court

## Type
- Region / Hidden Settlement (magical moon-elf court)
- Part of: Crevalon — within the Elantir Forest, accessed through a magical lake

## Description
- The Lunarian Court is a beautiful, otherworldly moonlit city hidden within the Elantir Forest; it is accessible only by being guided by a Lunarian or through the lake entrance.
- The court is the home of the Lunarians — a mysterious tribe of moon elves who worship Selune and serve their leader, Lady Kuyomi.
- Visitors who attempt to cross the lake uninvited may be stopped; the rule is that outsiders must be led by a Lunarian; Lady Kuyomi is actually trapped and cannot leave the court herself.
- The party in the earlier era was transported to the Lunarian Court within silver orbs taken from the Elantir Forest.
- The setting is a moonlit, magical city of elven design; it feels like stepping into another world.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The Court of the Lunarian Queen — where Lady Kuyomi holds court; described as having "honorable justices" who make formal introductions; procedurally formal with structured proceedings
- A recording sphere — a Lunarian artifact that can record and play back events; used during the party's visit

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Lady Kuyomi — the Lunarian leader; revealed to be a Solar (a celestial being of immense power); godlike in glory and power; a Solar's sword flies into the fray on its own; arrows from a Solar can strike a target dead on contact; she serves the Lunarian Queen (celestial forces); only twenty-four solars exist between all planes; she is physically trapped in the Elantir Forest/Court
- Haki — a Lunarian scout/warrior; a trusted member of the court and one of the first Lunarians the the earlier era party encounters in an organized capacity
- Unnamed Lunarian scouts and hunters — protect the forest; use silver orbs to transport outsiders; identified by their silver hair and luminescent skin
- The drow (the earlier era subplot) — drow who rebelled from the Underdark have aligned themselves with the Lunarians; the Lunarians enlisted their help to take out prominent Solarians

## History & Lore
- The Lunarians trace their origins to the DragonMage War era; the Lunarian Queen (a celestial being) commanded one faction of dragons (black, blue, green, silver, white) against the Solarian King's faction.
- The ancient Lunarian Queen and Solarian King struck a truce after the mages sealed the dragons; they divided day and night equally. The Lunarians have kept to themselves in the Elantir Forest since.
- During the earlier era: The Aviary staged a fake Lunarian attack on Finreld, disguising their operatives (Hummingbird, Ostrich, etc.) as Lunarian archers to provoke war between Finreld and the moon elves. This false flag was exposed when Florian Reiter detected that the attackers' blood did not taste Lunarian.
- The Lunarians do not bother Florian's vampire group in the forest as long as Florian's no-human-blood agreement holds.
- Kuyomi summoned the Aviary members (the drow mission referenced "she summoned them" to correspond with the Lunarians).
- A field study of the Lunarians was conducted by a famous elven mage long ago; the field notes are preserved in the library of the Sylvanian Academy in Handletare. The notes describe Lady Kuyomi as a Solar and record her powers.

## Connections
- Within the Elantir Forest in northern Crevalon
- Connected to Finreld (via the forest proximity; centaurs trade with Finreld)
- Connected to the Sylvanian Academy in Handletare (where field notes about the Lunarians are locked in the library)
- The Aviary (criminal organization) tried to provoke war between the Lunarians and Finreld
- The drow from the Underdark are aligned with the Lunarians after a rebellion

## Important Notes
- The Lunarians are MOON ELVES (a distinct type of elf) — NOT the same as drow (dark elves from the Underdark), NOT regular surface elves.
- Lady Kuyomi is revealed to be a SOLAR (a type of powerful celestial being) — NOT a vampire, NOT a mage, NOT a mortal queen.
- The Lunarian Court is HIDDEN within the Elantir Forest and accessible only through the magical lake — it is NOT a visible city on any ordinary map.
- The Lunarians worship SELUNE (the moon goddess) — NOT Torm, NOT Chauntea, NOT the Raven Queen.
- The Lunarians' leader (Lady Kuyomi / the Lunarian Queen) is TRAPPED and cannot leave the court — this is a significant limitation on their ability to act directly.
- The fake Lunarian attack on Finreld was the AVIARY's doing — the actual Lunarians did NOT attack Finreld.
