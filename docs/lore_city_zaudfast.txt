# Zaudfast

## Type
- City (naval base / coastal city)
- Part of: Crevalon (coastal, south/southwest region)

## Description
- Zaudfast is a coastal city in Crevalon that primarily operates as a naval base for the Crevalon Kingdom.
- The city has the feel of a military installation; most civilians avoided it during Campaign 1 due to rumors of vampires in the nearby Elantir Forest (though the forest is actually north, the rumors reached here).
- Garlic is strung in strings tied to the city walls — reflecting the vampire rumors and fear.
- The city's main tavern during Campaign 1 is The King's Chalice, essentially empty except for the owner Lilia due to the vampire rumors.
- Zaudfast is where Captain Karim (Fazia Karim) is based; she introduces the party to Commodore Julius Webb here.
- The Toven (Touven) Swamps lie south of Siadiff and near Zaudfast — the swamp area is close to the city.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The King's Chalice — tavern in Zaudfast; nearly empty during the vampire scare; run by Lilia
- Zaudfast Armory — a weapons/armory supplier in the city; the Babushka outfitted The Wench with 12 cannons purchased from the armory for 600 gold
- Crevalon Navy docks — the principal naval facility; where Crevalon warships including Captain Karim's vessel The Endeavor are based
- A travelling illusionist (wizard school dropout) lives/operates in Zaudfast during Campaign 1

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Captain Fazia Karim — Crevalon Navy officer; commands The Endeavor (later sunk); receives a replacement ship The Odyssey; a key NPC ally in Campaign 1; tieflings receive strange treatment in Zaudfast (social prejudice noted)
- Commodore Julius Webb — Karim's superior; full decorated uniform of blue and red with tasseled shoulders; he worries about Karim and clearly has some personal feeling toward her beyond professional concern
- Lilia — owner of The King's Chalice tavern
- Local soldiers and naval personnel

## History & Lore
- The party arrives at Zaudfast via ship (sailing The Wench alongside The Endeavor after a sea battle) in Campaign 1.
- Rumors of vampires in the region had emptied much of the civilian population; soldiers were stuck there by duty.
- Tales circulated in Zaudfast of a paladin who was "turned" in the nearby woods — referring to Florian Reiter; the age of stories lined up with when Florian was turned in the Elantir Forest.
- Zaudfast is a staging ground for adventures into the Elantir Forest during Campaign 1.
- During the orange and purple parade: the party enters Zaudfast in a parade with Lunarian escort, to the shock of the soldiers stationed there.

## Connections
- Coastal city in southern/southwestern Crevalon
- Near the Toven Swamps and close to Siadiff (south Crevalon)
- Connected to the Elantir Forest (north) via road and sea
- Connected to Aberdeen (the main western port) by sea
- Captain Karim operates from Zaudfast; she connects the naval subplot to the party's adventures

## Important Notes
- Zaudfast is a NAVAL BASE CITY — NOT a magic school city, NOT a paladin city (Siadiff is the paladin city). It is primarily a military installation.
- The Toven Swamps are near SIADIFF and ZAUDFAST (south Crevalon) — NOT near the Elantir Forest (north Crevalon).
- The vampire rumors in Zaudfast are about Florian Reiter's group in the Elantir Forest — but the forest itself is north, not immediately adjacent to Zaudfast.
- Tieflings receive unusual social treatment in Zaudfast ("gets treated strangely for being a tiefling" — referring to Karim).
