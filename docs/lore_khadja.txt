# Khadja

## Identity
- Nature/type: Djinn (genie)
- Race/species: Djinn; blue-skinned, tall, thin yet well-muscled
- Full name: Khadja (no surname given)
- Relation: Scirocco's mother; Duchess Jamila's daughter

## Role & Affiliation
- A djinn from the Plane of Air, originally inhabiting the City of Avis
- Was imprisoned within a ring for decades by Virion (Scirocco's father) after he became jealous and enraged
- Has since been freed from the ring by Lady Orvir's magic; now lives free on the Plane of Air in the City of Avis

## History & Origin
- Originally from the Plane of Air; yearned to return to the City of Avis ("endless skies and impressive structures built upon white clouds")
- Met Virion (a wizard) when he used a wish to spend a night with her; Khadja fell in love with him
- She became pregnant with Scirocco; Virion's attention drifted to another woman named Ophelia
- When Khadja confronted him ("You seem to have lost interest in me. Is it someone else?"), Virion replied cruelly: "In fact it is" — and imprisoned her within the ring, abandoning her
- After her imprisonment, an older member of a human desert oasis clan found the ring and summoned her; he wished for a night with her (the ring's magic bound her to grant such wishes)
- The ring was eventually buried with him in a tomb in the desert region near Adelanto
- Found by the party in the tomb (Session 31 onwards)

## Abilities & Powers
- Djinn powers: can conjure food and water out of thin air, limited wish-granting for whomever holds the ring
- Cannot use powers of her own volition — MUST be asked; she cannot cast Wish herself
- Her mother (Duchess Jamila) or grandfather could cast Wish on her behalf
- Cannot leave the ring until freed by a great mage breaking the enchantment, or until returned to the Plane of Air
- The ring is a command ring: if the djinni dies, the ring becomes nonmagical

## Key Relationships
- Scirocco: her daughter; she nearly cried upon seeing Scirocco for the first time in the ring ("I never thought that I would be able to see you again")
- Virion: Scirocco's father; the wizard who imprisoned her in the ring out of jealousy; she suspected he was infatuated with Ophelia
- Duchess Jamila: her mother; a djinn herself; descended from the Wind Dukes of Aaqa; lives in the City of Avis on the Plane of Air
- Lady Orvir: broke the enchantment on a similar ring imprisoning Lady Orvir's own mother (Gwentharidel); Khadja and Jamila were present for that reunion

## Key Events (previously)
- The party discovered her ring in a desert tomb (Session 31); Scirocco's mother materialized before them
- Khadja and Scirocco had a heart-to-heart in the inn in Adelanto; Khadja explained that Virion was indeed Scirocco's father and that he had imprisoned her shortly after giving birth
- Told Scirocco of the City of Avis and the Plane of Air; expressed longing to return
- Was present when a massive turtle-like beast (sent by Virion) attacked Adelanto; the party considered asking Khadja to fight alongside them
- Eventually brought to the Plane of Air: Scirocco and the party traveled there with Khadja and Jamila; Khadja showed them her favorite places in the City of Avis
- Was freed when Lady Orvir used powerful magic to break the ring's enchantment; finally returned to her home plane

## Important Notes
- Described: tall, thin yet well-muscled female with blue skin (like Scirocco's); dressed in airy, shimmering silks; speaks with a soft French accent in-game
- When she bows as a bound djinn, she wears "a deadened expression — Your wish is my command"
- The ring's demi-plane interior is a bare stone room with no windows or doors and a bedroll in a corner where she slept
- She is NOT the ring-bearer — the ring was held by various owners over the centuries; she was the one trapped within it
- Do not confuse Khadja's imprisonment ring with Lady Orvir's mother's ring — both were enchanted identically (Lady Orvir made that observation), but they are two separate people and two separate rings
