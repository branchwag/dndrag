# Emyelone

## Type
- City
- Part of: Crevalon (northern region)

## Description
- Emyelone is a northern Crevalon city with a reputation as an artful, culturally vibrant place, known for its poetry and a relaxed, permissive social atmosphere.
- Anton described it as having a very laissez-faire attitude.
- Raphael, the traveling performer the party encountered in Sharnwick, is from Emyelone.

## Notable Inhabitants
- Raphael — flamboyant performer and storyteller; wears a feathered purple cap and flowing purple and gold clothes; originally from Emyelone.
- Anton — also from Emyelone; gave the party their impression of the city.
- Fiona and Javen planned to move here to be together without judgment from others; they were later welcomed back to Finreld instead.

## Connections
- Northern Crevalon, distinct from the elven cities of Finreld and Felanor
