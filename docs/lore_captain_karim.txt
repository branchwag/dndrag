# Captain Fazia Karim

## Identity
- Nature/type: Naval officer / military captain
- Race/species: Tiefling (female)
- Full name: Captain Fazia Karim
- Confirmed female (she/her throughout)

## Role & Affiliation
- Captain in the Crevalon Royal Navy, commanding the naval ship The Endeavor
- Gunner aboard The Endeavor: Rodger Smith (also referenced as "Mr. Smith")
- previously

## History & Origin
- Grew up in an orphanage in Aberdeen
- Joined the military as soon as she was of age
- Described as "fiery but with the strongest sense of duty — she would not hesitate to risk her life for others"
- Was wounded in a battle against undead pirate Captain William McGee; carried a strange necrotic curse from the wound

## Abilities & Powers
- Skilled naval officer and swordswoman
- Keeps 2 potions of underwater breathing on her person at all times
- Issued orders in battle with authority; commanded her gunner to open fire on the giant squid

## Key Relationships
- Commodore Julius Webb: her superior officer; deeply concerned for her wellbeing after her injury; said of her: "She is our very best. Make sure she doesn't get herself killed"
- Sister Fiona: the elven healer of the Temple of Rao who saved Karim's life; Fiona moved Karim to her own room during recovery
- the party (Scirocco, Elly, Babushka, Wulfgar, and others): encountered during the pirate attack on The Golden Mermaid; became allied

## Key Events (previously)
- First appeared when The Endeavor pulled alongside The Golden Mermaid during a pirate mutiny; boarded the ship during the fight to assist the party
- Ordered Mr. Smith to fire the ship's cannons at an attacking giant squid, killing it and saving the party; declared The Golden Mermaid theirs afterward, with Babushka named Captain Babu
- Was captured by seafolk alongside the party; held in a magical bubble and dragged to an underwater arena city; looked "thoroughly furious" in her bubble
- Directed the party to sail toward Zaudfast following The Endeavor
- Was gravely wounded fighting undead pirate Captain William McGee; a necrotic curse negated all healing attempts, leaving her near death
- Carried to the Temple of Rao in Finreld, where Sister Fiona lifted the curse using Remove Curse and then healed her
- Her ship The Endeavor was sunk in the battle with McGee; most of her crew died; Commodore Webb arranged for a replacement ship (to be called The Odyssey)
- Present at the triple wedding at the campaign's end alongside the Commodore

## Important Notes
- Full name: Captain Fazia Karim (surname appears in a DM note: "forebears.io/surnames/karim")
- Plays with a British female voice in-game
- Tiefling identity: mentioned explicitly in session recap ("a tiefling" — "One of the healers scoffed at treating a tiefling")
- Do not confuse with Commodore Julius Webb, who is her superior officer, not her
- Not the same as any Ikovian naval character; she is Crevalon Royal Navy
