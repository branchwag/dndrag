# Ali Hassan

## Identity
- Wizard (Necromancy specialist)
- Race: Tiefling; described as "the tall tiefling gentleman... dressed in long red robes trimmed with gold"
- Voice described as implied from his appearance; "Necromancy — cosmic forces of life, death, and undeath"; married to Damania

## Role & Affiliation
- Head of Necromancy at Taelreth, Institute of the Arcane in Diondria
- He is the replacement for the previous head of necromancy — Virion Maganti — who was asked to leave approximately one year before Campaign 1 begins
- Philosophy: "Not everyone can teach necromancy in a way that is helpful rather than detrimental"; emphasizes "respect for the dead"
- Can command shadows that are capable of fighting (including potentially fighting a dragon)

## History & Origin
- Arrived at Taelreth after Virion Maganti was expelled; his arrival represents the school's attempt to restore legitimacy to its necromancy department
- Background details beyond his role at Taelreth are not detailed in the source material

## Abilities & Powers
- Head of Necromancy; necromantic spell repertoire at school-head level
- Can command shadows in combat (noted as a special ability: "can command shadows that at one point can fight the dragon")
- Performs Speaking with Dead when the party requires it (at Ophelia's request, assists with deciphering the diagrams involving speaking with dead)

## Key Relationships
- Ophelia: fellow academic at Taelreth; she calls on him when the party needs necromantic expertise ("Waves her hand in an arcane gesture to message Hassan — 'Hassan, do you have a moment?'")
- Virion Maganti: predecessor whose departure made room for Ali Hassan; Virion was asked to leave prior to Ali Hassan's arrival
- Evius Hallewell: fellow school head at Taelreth; they are colleagues
- The party (Campaign 1): met them when the party came to Taelreth; assisted with the speaking-with-dead diagrams

## Key Events (campaign)
- Campaign 1: Present at Taelreth when the party consulted the school heads; assisted with interpreting diagrams related to summoning devils (Speaking with Dead diagrams); appeared in the room at Ophelia's summons

## Important Notes
- Ali Hassan is explicitly the replacement for Virion Maganti as Head of Necromancy at Taelreth
- He is not a villain; his philosophy of "respect for the dead" contrasts directly with Virion's exploitative approach
- His school: Taelreth, Institute of the Arcane in Diondria (NOT the Sylvanian Academy in Handletare)
- Married to Damania (mentioned in DM notes alongside his character description)
