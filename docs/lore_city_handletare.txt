# Handletare

## Type
- City (elven city)
- Part of: Crevalon (northern region, across the river from Diondria)

## Description
- Handletare is an elven city in the north of Crevalon, situated across the river from Diondria; a ferry ride connects the two cities (described as less than an hour).
- The city is home to a prestigious elven magical academy — the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts — which is described as an elven West Point (a military-style magical academy with a chain of command and arcanists who function like officers).
- Felanor is described as being in a similar geographic zone ("close to your home," written to the Handletare-connected Ari, and placed roughly north of Finreld).
- Handletare has a ballet and opera scene; Florian took Caeda to a ballet in Handletare.
- The city has gardens and is considered a refined, cultured elven settlement.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts — an elven military-style magical academy; cathedral-like stone ceilings painted with scenes of the sky; headed by Lady Adrastea Orvir (Headmistress); the Head Arcanist is Rathilion Lorin, who works beneath her; functions like a military academy (chain of command, arcanists as officers)
- The Academy library — an immense room with ceilings so high they extend into darkness; walls entirely filled with books; enchanted floor constructs pathways under the visitor's feet as they walk; the books respond to concentrated thought to locate specific volumes
- A confectionary shop (nearby the Academy; used by Lady Orvir for meetings)
- A ballet venue in Handletare (where Florian takes Caeda)

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Lady Adrastea Orvir — noble; benefactor and overseer of the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts; donated her family's book collection to the Academy; studies planar magic; her mother Gwentharidel Orvir went missing on another plane; intelligent, poised, British-accented; actually a noble as well as a librarian/academic. Full name: Lady Adrastea Orvir; family name Orvir. Father: Erius Orvir (reclusive, obsessed with finding his missing wife; possibly based in the FrostLands as a wizard in a tower).
- Head Arcanist Rathilion Lorin — Head Arcanist at the Sylvanian Academy; works beneath Lady Orvir in the Academy hierarchy; NOT the overall leader
- Ari Crownsilver (previously) — invited to study at the Sylvanian Academy via a letter from Lord Florian Reiter; his father is described as a high elf academic in Handletare; Lady Vesemir's family connection to Ari suggests she could be his mother (she had a fling covered up)
- Adrastea Orvir (magic shop assistance) — also helps in her father's magic shop in Handletare in spare time
- Elven swordsmen at the Academy — train visiting students

## History & Lore
- Handletare is an ancient elven city; the Sylvanian Academy has been training elven and other magical students for generations.
- Lady Adrastea's ancestor, Minerva Orvir, died during the DragonMage War while serving the Lunarian Queen. The Orvir family has deep roots in Crevalon's magical history.
- The Academy's library contains tomes about the Lunarians, the DragonMage War, Avernus, and ancient magical knowledge — it is one of the most significant repositories of lore in Crevalon.
- Adrastea discovered that the Lady of Felanor (Lady Vesemir) protects one of the imprisoned dragons in a necklace gem. She is a descendant of one of the Mages who originally locked away the dragons.
- The Academy has extensive knowledge of planar magic; the party is directed to Handletare (or Diondria's north mage) for help with interplanar travel challenges.

## Connections
- Located across the river from Diondria; less than an hour's ferry ride
- Close to Felanor (a few weeks' journey or less by the description in letters)
- The Sylvanian Academy communicates with Finreld's noble house (elven connections)
- Lady Orvir's father Erius Orvir is connected to the FrostLands (he became a recluse there)
- The Academy's library contains locked tomes about the Lunarians; the party was directed to seek it out during the campaign
- Ari's father is a high elf academic in Handletare; Lady Vesemir is connected to Felanor and may be Ari's mother

## Important Notes
- The Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts is in HANDLETARE — NOT in Diondria. This is a critical disambiguation.
- Taelreth (the Institute of the Arcane) is in DIONDRIA — NOT Handletare. Two completely different institutions in two different cities.
- Lady Adrastea Orvir is the benefactor and overseer of the Sylvanian Academy in HANDLETARE. She is NOT at Taelreth.
- Handletare is an ELVEN city with a military-style academy; Diondria is a mixed-population city with a more conventional university-style institute.
- An Oathbow was crafted by the finest elven marksmen in Handletare (given to Pavel/Ari).
