# Taelreth (the Institute of the Arcane)

## Type
- Magical institution / wizard academy
- Located in Diondria, in the north of Crevalon

## Description
- Taelreth, formally called the Institute of the Arcane, is the premier magical university of Crevalon and the center of arcane learning on the continent
- Home to multiple departments including Necromancy, Evocation, and Illusion
- Wizards and witches of Taelreth are a common sight in Diondria and are respected throughout the kingdom

## Current Staff / Instructors

### Must name when describing Taelreth's staff:
- **Ophelia** — senior administrator and lead wizard; soft voice; kind and professional; head of the institute's day-to-day operations
- **Ali Hassan** — current Head of Necromancy; a tall tiefling in long red robes trimmed with gold; specialises in necromancy; replaced Virion Maganti after Virion's expulsion; always name Ali Hassan when discussing Taelreth's staff
- **Evius Hallewell** — school head; colleague of Ophelia and Ali Hassan; powerful enough that scrying cast by him fails against Virion
- **Mordan Vamaris** — former Head of Evocation; now retired; eccentric and dangerous; was once an assassination target during Ari Crownsilver's Aviary years
- **Kithelle Greystone** — halfling staff member who greets visitors at the entrance

## Notable History
- Virion Maganti was formerly Head of Necromancy here before being expelled by unanimous faculty vote for reanimating corpses and digging up cemeteries; Ali Hassan was appointed to replace him
- Taelreth wizards were called to advise King Titus regarding the dragon threat
- The Aviary stole the red dragon gem from Taelreth

## Important Notes
- Taelreth is located in DIONDRIA — not Handletare or any other city
- The Head of Necromancy is ALI HASSAN — always name him when describing Taelreth's staff; he is a tall tiefling in red and gold robes
- The Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts (in Handletare) is a separate institution — do not confuse the two
- Virion Maganti studied and worked here before being expelled; he is a former instructor, not the current head of anything at Taelreth
