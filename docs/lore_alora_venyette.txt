# Madame Alora Venyette

## Identity
- Ancient arcane mage and vampire (originally turned by a succubus from Avernus; was still a vampire when awakened; later allowed the party to make her human during the campaign after falling for Altarion; re-turned by Nikolai — an Ikovian changeling diplomat — after she and Altarion separated and she found being human unbearable)
- Race: Human (appears as a brunette woman with pale skin and sharpened nails/fangs in vampire form)
- Also referred to as "Madame Alora" in more recent times
- Alias: Court mage (former title in Crevalon, before King Titus's death and the Siadiff takeover)
- Full name on record: Alora Venyette (spelled "Venette" once in the text; primary spelling throughout is Venyette)

## Role & Affiliation
- Ancient era (~4000 years ago): one of the most powerful mages of her age; served Queen Elvira during the DragonMage War; used a purple gem as her arcane focus to seal a dragon; was subsequently betrayed by Queen Elvira, staked, and erased from the historical record
- After awakening: became court mage to King Titus of Crevalon after the party woke her from her centuries-long sealed sleep; this is a recent role, not her ancient one
- More recently: protects the Ikovian queen with an arcane shield; aligns with refugees seeking refuge in Ikovia
- Connected to the dwarves of Doragon — they were among those who assisted her after she sealed a dragon during the DragonMage War

## History & Origin

### Ancient era (~4000 years ago)
- Was originally turned into a vampire by a succubus from Avernus BEFORE the DragonMage War began; she had summoned the succubus to get information on the location of a missing colleague (Arcanus Loraine, a fellow scholar lost on another plane); the succubus overtook her during the exchange — she was already a vampire when the war started
- Her husband was still alive when she was first turned; he recognized the look in her eye and defended himself; over time she tried to find a cure
- Served Queen Elvira as a powerful mage during the DragonMage War; participated in sealing dragons using arcane rituals
- Queen Elvira led an independent faction of mages — they were NOT aligned with the Lunarian Queen or the Solarian King; the mage coalition acted separately from both dragon factions
- Queen Elvira ultimately betrayed her — siding with a dragon and using a force field to pin Alora to the wall; Alora was staked and her contributions to history were erased
- Lay sealed and forgotten for approximately 4000 years

### After awakening (service to King Titus)
- Awakened by a group of arcana users in Crevalon when the kingdom was in need; she was STILL A VAMPIRE when she awakened — she did not immediately become human
- Became court mage to King Titus of Crevalon during this period; served him and worked alongside the party as a vampire
- Fell for Altarion (the paladin who exorcised the pit fiend from her) during this period; he reminded her of her earlier husband and she developed genuine feeling for him despite disagreeing with how paladins treat people like her
- After developing feelings for Altarion, she allowed the party to change her back into a human — this was a choice she made during the campaign, influenced by her relationship with Altarion
- Found the human form deeply uncomfortable and unnatural; she described it as feeling fundamentally wrong
- After she and Altarion separated, her discomfort with being human intensified and became a central focus — she felt off, diminished, out of place in her own body
- Eventually sought to be re-turned; gathered arcane components in the Elantir Forest and met Nikolai there; was re-turned into a vampire by Nikolai (an Ikovian changeling diplomat) at the old manor in the Elantir Forest; she accepted his offer willingly after considering it carefully
- After being re-turned she and Nikolai developed a romantic connection
- She married Altarion at the campaign's end — though the marriage did not last

### More recently
- Rescued Nikolai from a werewolf attack in the Toven Swamps (near Siadiff)
- Traveled to Siadiff at King Titus's request to investigate wrongful imprisonment; was captured by Sir Thomas Wright (possessed by an infernal being) and subjected to days of radiant torture in adamantine cuffs
- Nikolai (disguised as Sir Thomas) broke her out; they traveled to Ikovia together to warn of Siadiff's growing corruption

## Abilities & Powers
- Powerful arcane mage; specializes in planar knowledge and arcane research
- Can cast invisibility, teleportation (plane-level precision), scrying, and complex arcane rituals
- Participated in sealing a dragon using a purple gem as arcane focus during the DragonMage War
- As a vampire, has enhanced senses, strength, and fangs; previously spent centuries in enchanted sleep and is not weakened by sunlight in the manner of standard vampires
- Carries a sending stone

## Key Relationships
- Nikolai: Ikovian changeling diplomat who re-turned her; became her romantic partner; she rescued him from the Toven Swamps after a werewolf attack
- Florian Reiter: they share memories of the old Elantir Forest manor; she remembered their past conversation on the stairwell about his nature, and the anger she encountered from Caeda there
- Caeda: she encountered her at the manor and remembered Caeda's anger toward her
- Altarion: she had complicated feelings toward him — he reminded her of her earlier husband, toward whom she has a soft spot, but she did not agree with how paladins treat people like her; the prejudice was on Altarion's side, and she proved him wrong over the course of the campaign; when possessed by the pit fiend she attacked him (96 Blight damage) and his exorcism burned her face; they married at the campaign's end; the marriage did not work out
- Queen Elvira: previous queen who defended Alora but eventually sided with a dragon, which led to a falling out and a pivotal betrayal; the Queen used a force field to pin Alora to the wall
- Virion Maganti: he tampered with a shard she was connected to, causing her to be possessed
- The dwarves of Doragon: connected to her as allies who assisted her after the dragon-sealing ritual
- Sir Thomas Wright: she sensed a darkness in him — "a dual nature, a shadow over his countenance, a glint of red within his eye" — during their first meeting; he later captured and tortured her while infernally possessed

## Key Events
Approximately 4000 years ago, Alora was already a vampire when the DragonMage War began — she had been turned by a succubus of Avernus before the war. She served Queen Elvira, who led an independent mage coalition separate from both the Lunarian and Solarian factions. Alora participated in sealing a dragon using a purple gem at the summit of an icy peak. The dwarves of Doragon revived her with smelling salts and animal blood afterward. She was later betrayed by Queen Elvira, staked, and erased from the historical record — lying sealed for millennia.

She was awakened by a group of arcana users in Crevalon — still a vampire when she awoke. She served King Titus as court mage during this period. She fell for Altarion, the paladin who had exorcised a pit fiend from her, and chose to allow the party to change her back into a human. She found the human form deeply uncomfortable and unnatural. After she and Altarion separated, her discomfort with being human became a central preoccupation — she felt fundamentally off, out of place in her own body. She eventually sought to be re-turned. She gathered arcane components in the Elantir Forest, where she met Nikolai; she accepted his offer and was re-turned at Florian's old manor in the forest, with their relationship developing from there.

She rescued Nikolai from a werewolf attack in the Toven Swamps, then traveled to Siadiff at King Titus's request to investigate wrongful imprisonment. There she was captured by Sir Thomas Wright — infernally possessed at the time — and subjected to days of radiant torture in adamantine cuffs. Nikolai infiltrated Siadiff disguised as Thomas Wright and broke her out. They traveled to Ikovia together to warn of Siadiff's growing corruption.

In more recent times, Alora has been seen protecting the Ikovian queen with an arcane shield, standing alongside Nikolai. She traveled through tunnels with a group of refugees and was greeted — and briefly punched — by Adelonda upon arriving in Ikovia.
