# Lady Vesemir (Lady Endalia Jaeraerith Vesimir)

## Identity
- Noblewoman and lady of the elven city of Felanor
- Race: Elf
- Full name referenced: "Lady Endalia Jaeraerith Vesimir" (from the DM notes about the gem and its connection to "Daereis Vesemir — Otherwise known as The Third Daughter of Corellon")
- Surname variant spellings in source: "Vesemir," "Vesimir," "Vesemirs" — all referring to the same family/character
- Also associated with a notable ballerina within her circle: "Lady Vesemir Almithara, the famous ballerina" is mentioned at the masquerade (possibly a different Lady Vesemir, or a relative)

## Role & Affiliation
- Lady of Felanor (the Green City); described as the "middle-age daughter of the late Lord and Lady Vesemir"
- Hosts an annual masquerade ball at her manor outside Felanor; the event draws aristocracy from across Crevalon and Finreld
- Guardian (unknowing or knowing) of an ancient imprisoned dragon: the green dragon, sealed 4,000 years ago by her ancestor Daereis Vesemir

## History & Origin
- Her ancestor Daereis Vesemir ("The Third Daughter of Corellon") was one of the mages who sealed the ancient green dragon 4,000 years ago using a gem necklace
- Lady Vesemir has inherited this gem necklace and wears it each year at her masquerade
- She has not married; described as "has not married" in the DM notes
- She appears to have a close reading relationship and a scholarly character: told Elly "I would assume you've read every book in Crevalon by now!"

## Abilities & Powers
- No specific magical abilities described; she is a noblewoman, not a mage
- The green dragon is sealed inside the gem necklace she wears

## Key Relationships
- Lady Adrastea Orvir: moves in the same social circle; both attend each other's events; Florian met Lady Orvir at one of Lady Vesemir's parties ("her and I met in Felanor a few years prior at one of Lady Vesimir's parties")
- Florian Reiter: attends Lady Vesemir's annual parties without fail; Florian commented that Caeda looked "strikingly similar to the Vesemirs"
- Caeda: possible hidden family connection — DM note reads "Lady Vesemir could be her mom — she had a fling that was covered up"; Florian noticed the resemblance
- The party (previously): hosted them at her masquerade; Florian was hunched over her during a medical emergency at the ball
- Rathilion Lorin: present at her masquerade

## Key Events (campaign)
- previously: Hosted the masquerade ball at her Felanor manor; the Aviary targeted the event to steal the green dragon gem from her necklace; the party was sent to protect her; during the ball, Florian's vampirism was exposed when he went to help Lady Vesemir during some kind of episode; Sir Godwin apprehended Florian there with radiant whips

## Important Notes
- The gem necklace she wears at her annual masquerade contains the sealed ancient green dragon; she may or may not be fully aware of this
- The Aviary targeted her masquerade specifically because of this gem
- The DM note about Caeda's parentage ("Lady Vesemir could be her mom") is not confirmed in-game; treat as a possible fact, not established fact
- Her ancestor is specifically "Daereis Vesemir — The Third Daughter of Corellon" who sealed the green dragon 4,000 years ago; this lineage is what makes the necklace significant
- She is a noble elven lady of Felanor, NOT a mage or arcanist; her importance is her guardian status over the green dragon gem
