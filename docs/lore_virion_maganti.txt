# Virion Maganti

## Identity
- Wizard; in the process of becoming a lich (lich ritual partially complete as of previously end; described as a "lich/mummy lord mix" for combat purposes)
- Race: Human (exact race not specified beyond being a mortal mage)
- Full name: Virion Maganti
- The main villain of previously

## Role & Affiliation
- Former head of Necromancy at Taelreth, Institute of the Arcane in Diondria (before Ali Hassan's arrival; left approximately one year before Ali Hassan took the position)
- Was asked to leave Taelreth via a unanimous faculty vote for digging up local cemeteries, reanimating corpses, and pursuing research considered to go "too far"
- After leaving Taelreth: operates from a desert tomb in the Slaerian Desert; also has multiple bases (including a wizard's tower in the north — he murdered the wizard who lived there and took it over)
- Aligned with Bel, the Warlord of Avernus, via a deal: Bel promised to turn Virion into a lich with a normal (non-rotting) body in exchange for Virion helping facilitate the fallen celestials (the Solarian King and Lunarian Queen) becoming irredeemable and being taken to hell, which would give Bel two celestials to tip the Blood War against the demons
- Commands legions of undead
- Has an imp as a familiar
- Summoned Silvester (a pit fiend servant of Bel) through his experiments

## History & Origin
- Father was a farmer from Sharnwick who traveled town to town selling goods; bandits attacked and killed him on his route; no one was ever brought to justice for the crime
- Virion became obsessed with raising his father from the dead (hence his necromantic pursuits) and with finding the killers; was sickened that no one cared about his father's death
- While at Taelreth he fell in love with Ophelia (a faculty member); she did not return his feelings; he took this rejection bitterly, feeling she "didn't want to see him succeed"
- Was asked to leave by unanimous faculty vote (including Ophelia); he stormed out and disappeared
- Scirocco is his daughter: he used a censer to summon Khadja (a djinn from the Plane of Air), forced her to give up their child Scirocco, and then imprisoned Khadja within a ring
- Encountered and feebleminded Gwentharidel Orvir (Lady Orvir's mother) on another plane, trapping her there; Gwen had gotten caught up in the conflict while trying to find Khadja
- A group of paladins from Siadiff (Florian's group) went to hunt Virion; all of them were presumed dead/failed

## Abilities & Powers
- Powerful enough that scrying fails against him (Evius Hallewell's scrying cannot penetrate his defenses)
- Commands undead legions
- Has a phylactery-style arrangement with Bel: Bel keeps him alive until their deal is fulfilled; if the lich ritual were to fail, Bel would take Virion's soul to the afterlife
- Intended phylactery: a ring set with a sapphire that he planned to give Ophelia once he won her heart (though note: "No phylactery bc that's part of his deal with Bel/Silvester")
- Wears an anti-scrying item to prevent detection
- Has reanimated a red dragon as a dracolich
- Can reanimate mummy lords and other powerful undead
- Uses Solomon's skull (Speaking with Dead) to locate the other imprisoned dragons easily

## Key Relationships
- Scirocco: his biological daughter (by Khadja the djinn); he does not acknowledge the relationship and imprisoned Khadja to prevent her from being a weakness to him
- Khadja: the djinn he imprisoned in a ring after she gave birth to Scirocco; she suspected he was infatuated with Ophelia
- Ophelia: the woman he loved and was rejected by; her rejection is one of the core wounds driving his villainy
- Bel / Silvester: Virion made a deal with Bel (via Silvester, Bel's pit fiend servant) to gain lichdom with a normal body
- The Bluejay (a changeling Aviary member): she served as a go-between; Virion convinced her to approach Kuyomi and offer the Aviary's assistance in awakening dragons
- Ange the fortune teller: Silvester was released through Virion's experiments but used Ange as a weaker subject host rather than stay under Virion's control
- Lady Orvir / Adrastea: he is connected to her mother Gwen's disappearance (feebleminded and trapped on another plane)

## Key Events (campaign)
- Orchestrated the conflict between Solarians and Lunarians to further his deal with Bel (needed the fallen celestials to become irredeemable)
- Reanimated the mummy lord Solomon for his purposes; stole Solomon's skull to use with Speaking with Dead to locate imprisoned dragons
- Directed the Aviary (through the Bluejay) to steal the red dragon gem from Taelreth
- Released a red dragon; Florian killed it
- Was found at his desert tomb by the party; Feebleminded party members during the encounter
- Had multiple hideouts including the desert tomb and a wizard's tower in the Frostlands (he murdered the tower's former occupant)
- Bel secretly plans to have the lich ritual fail and take Virion's soul to the afterlife once the deal is done

## Important Notes
- His age is unclear given his current (partially lich) state
- He is described as NOT yet a full lich at the start of the campaign; he is "in the process of becoming a lich"
- He is the son of Charles Maganti (a Sharnwick farmer killed by bandits); Virion's motivation stems from his father's murder and the world's indifference to it
- "Virion is the same [as The Bluejay]. No one cared about his plight." — The Bluejay's words suggest he and she share a parallel wound
- The Bluejay has information about "where Virion keeps the keys"
- Bel's involvement means Virion's fate is ultimately determined by a devil who never intended to honor the deal
