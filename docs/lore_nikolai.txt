# Nikolai

## Identity
- Nature/type: Changeling and vampire — Nikolai is a changeling who is also a vampire; always describe him using both terms
- Race/species: Changeling / Vampire
- Full name: Nikolai (surname not given; introduced himself simply as "Nikolai, Ikovian relations")

## Role & Affiliation
- Ikovian diplomat; traveled to Crevalon to seek an audience with the King on behalf of Ikovia
- Became Alora Venyette's romantic partner and the vampire who re-turned her
- Appears in the Alora novella/backstory document and in more recently

## History & Origin
- Met Alora in the Elantir forest while hunting; she was gathering arcane components and witnessed him feed on a deer, then revealed herself rather than hide
- Had traveled from Ikovia to Crevalon to warn or seek an audience, hoping for a diplomatic meeting with the King
- An Ikovian, with a notable Ikovian accent
- As a changeling vampire, he could shift his appearance; in more recently he used this ability to impersonate a paladin in order to rescue Alora from danger and escape Crevalon

## Abilities & Powers
- Vampire abilities: feeding, superhuman senses, blood bond (telepathic connection with those he turns)
- Changeling shapeshifting: can alter his physical appearance to convincingly pass as another person, including a paladin
- The blood bond / re-turning process: after feeding on Alora to the point of near-death, she rested in a coffin and rose as a vampire; their turning created a temporary telepathic tether

## Key Relationships
- Alora Venyette: he re-turned her from human back to vampire after she asked him to; they formed a romantic relationship
- Florian Reiter: Alora's old ally; after re-turning, Alora visited the manor that had once been Florian's, and Nikolai was present ("Come in," Nikolai said softly)
- Yuri Laskin (more recently): not the same individual; Nikolai is the Ikovian diplomat who re-turned Alora, while Yuri is a separate vampire with a different history

## Key Events (Alora Novella)
- Encountered Alora hunting in the Elantir forest; she revealed herself and they introduced themselves
- Discussed her former vampirism and her regret at having been "cleansed" back to human; offered to re-turn her if she wished
- Alora initially hesitated; returned to his inn room days later and accepted; they arranged to meet at Florian's abandoned manor in the forest
- At the manor, performed the re-turning: drained her blood to the point of death; she lay in a coffin and rose the next night as a vampire again
- After the re-turning, fed her his blood so she could complete the transition; she described the experience as long-overdue relief
- Later in the story, Alora used a scrying orb to check on Nikolai and found him gravely injured — limbs severed, deep gashes, near-dead in a pool of vampiric blood beside an overturned carriage in the woods; she teleported to him and nursed him back with a crimson potion and her own blood
- Was settling at Florian's old manor with Alora between scenes (he is present when Alora returns from a mission, and their sending stones are linked)

## Key Events (more recently)
- Appears in the tunnels beneath the mountains alongside Alora and other refugees fleeing Crevalon
- Used his changeling ability to disguise himself as a paladin to save Alora; the party witnesses him shift form: "You watch as his form morphs — from the visage of a paladin to that of an abnormally pale man, paler than even the vampires you've been..."
- Described as injured in more recently; is present with Alora in the refugee tunnel scene
- Listed in more recently necromancer escort names (a different Nikolai — "Nikolai Varkov" — appears in a list of necromancers; this is not the same person as Alora's Nikolai)

## Important Notes
- Do not confuse "Nikolai Varkov" (a named necromancer in a list of Ikovian military necromancers) with this Nikolai; this Nikolai is Alora's vampire companion and an Ikovian diplomat
- Do not confuse with Yuri Laskin, who is Milly's mentor and a different vampire
- His changeling nature is subtle — Alora perceived "something more to him" when she first saw him, picking up on the changeling trait before she knew what it was
- He is confirmed as Alora's romantic partner based on the novella text; referred to as "her new boo" in more recently DM notes ("Alora and Nikolai — seeking refuge in Ikovia")
- His surname is never given in the available text
