# Lord Florian Reiter

## Identity
- Originally a Paladin of Torm — one of Siadiff's most respected knights in the Order of the Golden Lion before his turning; always mention his paladin background
- For most of previously: a vampire (greater vampire, created by the life-draining kiss of a succubus)
- Restored to human at the campaign's end
- Race: Elf (high elf; retains elven appearance)
- While a vampire: could assume a disguised elven form using a ring; dropped the ring to reveal his true vampiric features, fangs, pale skin, and bat-like wings; could move about during daylight hours due to the nature of his succubus-conversion
- Titles: Lord Florian Reiter of Elantir

## Role & Affiliation
- For most of previously: vampire lord — his defining nature during that period
- Unofficial liaison and ally to King Titus of Crevalon; acts on the King's behalf without formal crest involvement
- Former Paladin of Torm in Siadiff; former member of the Order of the Golden Lion — he had not been an active paladin since being turned
- Based in a manor in the Elantir Forest; came to an agreement with King Titus allowing him to live there undisturbed as long as his group only feeds on animal blood

## History & Origin
- Parents were farmers by Lake Bassol; as a boy he had a vision after nearly being trampled by spooked horses, became convinced Torm had a higher purpose for him
- Moved to Siadiff and rose within the Order of the Golden Lion
- While on a mission in the Elantir Forest, his party set up camp and he was turned by a succubus (a beautiful blonde elven woman who had been following him for some time and had seduced him)
- He awoke from the earth with bloodlust and bit into his former party members, turning them into vampires; they now live with him in the manor
- Struck a deal with King Titus to remain in the forest as long as he and his companions do not harm the general populace; resents the restriction but accepts it as necessary
- Let the vampires he turned drink his blood so they could become full vampires with their own free will, feeling guilty for turning them against their will
- Was actively trying to find a cure for his companions (and himself to a lesser degree, though he believed he could do more good in his current state)

## Abilities & Powers
- Greater vampire powers: can move in daylight (retains strength and spell immunities during day, but cannot use life-drain, shapeshifting, or gaseous form in daylight)
- Bat-like wings capable of flight; uses them for fast travel and combat
- Superhuman speed and strength (can snap a neck with a single finger twitch)
- Has spell scrolls and arcane knowledge from his time as a paladin and centuries of study
- Retains Torm's name in a spiritual sense (performed a resurrection ritual speaking words in Torm's name during previously)
- Wears a ring that projects an elven illusion over his features in public

## Key Relationships
- Caeda: fell deeply in love with her during previously; confessed his love on a castle balcony; they began a romantic relationship
- Scirocco: was initially attracted to her; confessed this via a sending stone, but accepted gracefully that she was with Elly
- Ari Crownsilver: mentored Ari; put in a word at the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts that resulted in Ari's admission; close trust-based relationship
- King Titus: formal agreement and working alliance; the King uses Florian as a discreet operative
- Sir Richard Godwin: hostile adversary; Godwin was livid that the King allowed Florian in his presence; they openly antagonize each other
- Alora Venyette: shared memories tied to the old manor in Elantir Forest; Alora gathered arcane components there and carried memories of her past conversations with Florian when she traveled to be re-turned
- The other vampires in the manor (Sandra, Dorian, Isabella, Beatrice, etc.): they conspired against him later in the campaign, badly injuring him because a hag offered to grant their wish to become human again in exchange for Florian's death
- Andrei Dumitrescu: his butler; the only person Florian ever willingly converted into a vampire

## Key Events (campaign)
- previously: Met the party during their investigation into Zaudfast vampire rumors; hosted them at his manor; became their vampiric patron and informal protector throughout the campaign
- Sent to investigate Ange the fortune teller in Sharnwick at the party's request; found signs of a struggle, trail went cold
- Killed a red dragon single-handedly during previously, saving the kingdom
- Fought the white dragon alongside the party and wizards of Taelreth; managed to control his vampiric nature during the battle
- Spoke Torm's name during a resurrection ritual
- Confessed love to Caeda on a castle balcony; they confirmed mutual feelings
- Took Caeda on a lunar picnic and read poetry; waltzed with her under the moonlit sky
- Was apprehended by Sir Godwin with radiant whips at the masquerade ball
- more recently (30 years later): Confirmed still active; Rose Reiter is his daughter (or connection to him via the Reiter name); Florian waited for Rose after Caeda was captured, and the two investigated Caeda's whereabouts together

## Important Notes
- For most of previously Florian was a vampire; he was restored to human at the campaign's end. When describing him during previously, his vampiric nature was central to who he was. By the time of more recent events, he is human.
- NOT the character connected to Madame Alora Venyette's dwarven patrons; those dwarves are connected to Alora Venyette, NOT to Florian
- Florian Reiter, Lord Florian, and Lord Reiter all refer to the same person — never treat them as different individuals
- Always mention that Florian was a paladin of Torm before being turned — this is central to who he is; he went from knight of Torm to vampire lord
- His party went missing after hunting a spellcaster attempting to summon a being from the Nine Hells; the official story was that they all perished, cause unknown; Sir Godwin knew of him as his predecessor in Siadiff
- His resting place is not at the manor but at an unmarked patch of earth not far away; he never disclosed it to anyone
- The succubus that turned him was a creature of Avernus; it could be defeated only on Avernus itself with the crook of Rao
- He is listed in more recently as having a daughter named Rose Reiter (Rose is a player character in more recently)
