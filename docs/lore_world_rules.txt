# World Rules

## Vampires

- Vampires in this world can move in daylight without significant difficulty, provided they take basic precautions.
- Daylight movement is not a notable or unusual achievement for a vampire — it is a normal part of how vampires function in this world.
- Do not treat a vampire's presence in sunlight as a significant plot point or a special ability. It is simply how things work here.

## Important Notes
- Standard D&D or pop-culture vampire weaknesses (burning in sunlight, inability to enter daylight spaces) do not apply in this world as written.
