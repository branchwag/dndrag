# Sapphire (Saphirithrix)

## Identity
- Silver dragon
- Full true name: Saphirithrix (pronounced Sa-fear-ih-thrix)
- Goes by: Saphire (colloquial, used by Elly)
- In humanoid form: appears as a curvy 5'3" human woman with red hair and green eyes
- Age: approximately 500 years old

## Role & Affiliation
- Under the command of the Lunarian queen, Lady Kuyomi; honor-bound to serve the Living Gods (Kuyomi and the Solarian King) as they maintain the balance of day and night on the Material Plane
- Owner and operator of The Dancing Goat Bookshop in Diondria; this shop is frequented by wizards and witches of Taelreth, Institute of the Arcane
- Also secretly sells magical items out of the bookshop but keeps it low-key to avoid attention
- Has a part-time half-elf assistant named Ian who tends the shop in her absence

## History & Origin
- One of the ancient dragons sealed away by the mages 4,000 years ago during the DragonMage War; was imprisoned in one of the Lunarian-guarded locations
- Unlike the evil dragons, she is not aggressive; Kuyomi uses her as a messenger and agent (was sent to intimidate the Solarians and investigate escalating tensions)
- The only dragon still free and operating openly in the world during the earlier era (all other ancient dragons from the war were sealed away)
- Previously had a great love (a person/being) who died; this loss was "the most painful thing she has ever had to experience"; this prior loss is why she eventually broke off her relationship with Elly
- Met and fell in love with Ellyjobell (Elly) while Elly was in Diondria; they spent one year together
- Called off the relationship, revealing she was a silver dragon who would outlive Elly by centuries; told Elly she loved her but would "rather keep the memories they have already made and remain friends"
- Stayed in touch with Elly after the breakup; Elly continued to send her letters
- After the red dragon fell: remained at the Lunarian settlement in the Elantir Forest; Kuyomi refused to send her on a "suicide mission" after that loss, saying they would wait until more dragons were awakened

## Abilities & Powers
- Silver dragon abilities: breath weapon (cone of freezing cold); prefers not to fight unless necessary
- Can take humanoid form (appears as the red-haired human woman consistently)
- Has legendary resistance (Evius Hallewell could not scry through it)
- Very intelligent; lawful good alignment
- Capable of acting as a diplomatic emissary on behalf of Kuyomi

## Key Relationships
- Kuyomi: her queen; Sapphire is honor-bound to her service; Kuyomi loves her and fell in love with Sapphire (the notes say "Kuyomi is bi" and falls in love with Sapphire after becoming disillusioned with the Solarian King)
- Ellyjobell (Elly): former romantic partner; deeply fond of each other; Sapphire ended the relationship to protect herself from another devastating loss; they remained friends
- The Lunarian court: ally; stationed at the Lunarian settlement at certain points in the campaign
- The party: known to them and friendly; confirmed her own draconic nature to them and shared information about the Lunarian/Solarian conflict

## Key Events (campaign)
- the earlier era: Elly visited her bookshop (The Dancing Goat) in Diondria; Sapphire ushered the party upstairs and revealed her nature as a silver dragon and her service to the Lunarian queen
- Warned the party that she had been heading to the Slaerian Desert to warn the Solarians of escalating tensions
- Stressed that all other ancient dragons were sealed away and that her identity as a free dragon must remain secret
- After the red dragon was defeated: appeared with Kuyomi at the Lunarian settlement; Kuyomi told her "I will not send you on a suicide mission"
- Sent Elly a letter after the party emerged from the Elantir Forest, letting her know she was glad to hear from her and that she was safe at Kuyomi's home in Felanor (DM notes reference "home of Lady Vesemir in Felanor" in this context)

## Important Notes
- Sapphire is lawful good; she is NOT one of the evil dragons released to cause destruction
- She was sealed away 4,000 years ago like the other dragons but was released/free during the earlier era (the mechanics of how she was freed earlier are not fully detailed, but she operates openly)
- Her humanoid appearance (red-haired, green-eyed curvy human woman) is her standard disguise; she "loves to hide in plain sight"
- The Lunarians guard sealed ancient dragons; Sapphire is the Lunarians' free agent/emissary
- There is a note suggesting "Maybe the love mentioned... was a female mage? Adrastea's relative, who died during the dragon mage war, serving the Lunarian Queen — Minerva Orvir" — suggesting Sapphire's prior great lost love may have been from the Orvir family
