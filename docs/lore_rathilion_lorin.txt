# Rathilion Lorin

## Identity
- Arcanist / Wizard
- Race: Elf (implied; associated with the elven institution in Handletare)
- Title: Head Arcanist at the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts

## Role & Affiliation
- Head Arcanist at the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts in Handletare
- Second in command (or highest-ranked academic) under Lady Adrastea Orvir, who is the Benefactor and Overseer of the Academy
- There is "another Arcanist below Rathilion" mentioned in the hierarchy

## History & Origin
- Details of his personal history are not provided in the source material beyond his role at the Academy

## Abilities & Powers
- "Arcanist Rathilion Lorin for example has quite beautiful techniques" — described as having impressive magical technique by Lady Orvir herself
- No specific spell details provided

## Key Relationships
- Lady Adrastea Orvir: his superior; the Academy's Benefactor and Overseer; she spoke of his beautiful techniques admiringly
- The party (the earlier era): met him at Lady Vesemir's masquerade ball; he was wearing a lion mask at the masquerade

## Key Events (campaign)
- the earlier era: Was present at Lady Vesemir's masquerade ball, where he was speaking with Lady Adrastea Orvir when the party arrived

## Important Notes
- Rathilion Lorin is at the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts in Handletare, NOT at Taelreth in Diondria
- His title is Head Arcanist, NOT head of a school specialization like the Taelreth department heads
- He is subordinate to Lady Orvir in the Academy hierarchy
