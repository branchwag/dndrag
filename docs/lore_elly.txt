# Ellyjobell (Elly)

## Identity
- Deep gnome (Svirfneblin); 57 years old at the start of Campaign 1 (described as "youngish" for a gnome)
- Full name: Ellyjobell; goes by "Elly"
- Warlock (fey patron); later also pledges herself to Rao and wields a greatsword
- Player character in Campaign 1
- In a romantic relationship with Scirocco during Campaign 1

## Role & Affiliation
- Informal agent of King Titus alongside the rest of the Campaign 1 party
- "Sister Elly" — Sir Godwin uses this address, implying she is recognized as an affiliate of the Order of Rao or the sisterhood in Siadiff at some point
- Offered and accepted the position of heading up the Temple of Rao (after Campaign 1 events); accepted this offer "in a moment of speechless surprise"
- Was formerly associated with The Aviary (Bink, her former lover, was later recruited to The Aviary as The Robin)

## History & Origin
- Born and raised in the Underdark
- In her 30s, fell in love with Brooke Bingles ("Bink"), another female deep gnome from the Underdark; loved her deeply but Bink rejected her, saying she did not have enough possessions and gold to settle down with
- Left the Underdark to earn wealth to win Bink back; reached Diondria
- In Diondria, met Saphirithrix (Sapphire; silver dragon in humanoid form) at The Dancing Goat Bookshop; they began a relationship that lasted approximately one year; Sapphire ended it, citing that she would outlive Elly by centuries and could not bear another heartbreak; they remain friends
- After leaving Sapphire, was wandering in the woods and encountered a fey who she believed was Titania; made repeated advances until the fey gave her warlock powers to make her stop, and then assigned her tasks; Elly mistakenly believed she had won the fey's affection
- The fey she met was NOT Titania — it was the Queen of Air and Darkness posing as Titania (Titania is her sister); Elly does not learn this until later in Campaign 1

## Abilities & Powers
- Warlock abilities: fey patron spells and invocations; Eldritch Blast and other warlock signature spells
- Later wields a greatsword in addition to her warlock powers (picked up by training with Rao's paladins; described as wielding it "powerfully despite her small stature")
- Her warlock powers come from the Queen of Air and Darkness (not Titania); therefore her powers are associated with the Unseelie Court, not the Seelie Court
- When she tried to attack Lunarians with her warlock powers, they did not work — her patron's power is in opposition to Kuyomi's moon-aligned magic
- Pledged herself to Rao during Campaign 1; her connection to Rao grows as the campaign progresses; Rao visits her in a dream to say he is proud of her
- Has fortune-telling flair: when she does a dramatic reading for someone it produces a memorable line ("you will die in 7 days...just kidding")

## Key Relationships
- Scirocco: her romantic partner during Campaign 1; Scirocco proposed with a ring woven from orange mountain flowers; Elly had made the first (very forward) proposal to Scirocco on the ship before this
- Sapphire (Saphirithrix): former romantic partner; they remain friends; Sapphire's cat Mollymauk was Elly's companion during Campaign 1; Elly was overjoyed when Mollymauk was recovered after being taken
- Bink (Brooke Bingles): Elly's unrequited love from the Underdark; later recruited into The Aviary as The Robin; Elly did not know this at the start of Campaign 1
- The Dove (fey patron, believed to be Titania): the Queen of Air and Darkness disguised as Titania; she gave Elly warlock powers and assigned her tasks; she asked Elly to kill Florian (Elly refused); Elly eventually discovers The Dove's true identity
- Rao: god of peace and reason; she pledged herself to Rao during Campaign 1 and received his blessing; Rao visited her in a dream expressing pride in her
- Florian Reiter: the patron asked Elly to slay him; Elly refused; the group debated this at length; Elly's relationship with Florian is complicated but ultimately collaborative
- Sir Godwin: he sends his regards to "Sister Elly" via Altarion; indicates a respectful connection through shared Siadiff/Rao ties
- Mollymauk: Sapphire's cat; traveled with Elly; was taken during Campaign 1 and then recovered (The Bluejay brought Mollymauk back); Elly rejoiced at the reunion

## Key Events (campaign)
- Campaign 1: Present from the start in Sharnwick; received fey patron dream while on the road
- Was the first to offer herself as a soul sacrifice to reinforce the celestial barrier (the group refused this)
- Her fey patron asked her to kill Florian; she refused; this created tension and a long debate about whether her actions were her own or her patron's; the party debated whether she should renounce her patron
- Was told in a dream by Titania (actually the Queen of Air and Darkness) that "my sister" (Titania) has recruited one of her friends (Bink/Robin) and is recruiting more
- Communed with her patron and discovered The Dove was none other than the Queen of Air and Darkness herself
- Encountered The Dove in person: "an ethereal-looking fey being and the sister of Titania"; the Dove showed Elly a vision of Virion's hideout in exchange for cooperation
- Held Virion in place while Scirocco breathed lightning on him — a key moment in defeating Virion's physical form
- Accepted the position of heading the Temple of Rao after Campaign 1
- Cleansed Gwen (Lady Orvir's mother) of the feeblemind spell "with Rao and the power of friendship"

## Important Notes
- Elly's fey patron is NOT Titania — it is the Queen of Air and Darkness disguised as Titania; this is one of the most significant plot twists of Campaign 1
- The Queen of Air and Darkness is also The Dove, the leader of The Aviary — Elly's patron is therefore also the head of the criminal organization the party was fighting against
- Her Underdark hometown is close to where Virion's hideout was discovered (a near-coincidence that the party noticed)
- She is sapphic/queer — her significant relationships in Campaign 1 are with Sapphire (a woman), Bink (a woman), and Scirocco (a woman)
- "Sister Elly" — Siadiff considers her a sister despite her warlock patron; this may be because of her demonstrated faith in Rao
- After Campaign 1, she heads a Temple of Rao — this is a major shift from warlock to divine servant
- Her warlock powers do not work against Lunarians (Kuyomi's moon-aligned faction): the Queen of Air and Darkness's power is opposed to the Lunarian celestial essence
