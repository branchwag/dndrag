# The Lady Finger

## Type
- Sailing ship (party vessel, previously)

## Description
- The Lady Finger is the sailing ship acquired and used by the party.
- Named The Lady Finger; flies a pink flag bearing cupcake skulls and crossbones with a chef's hat.
- Equipped with cannons; the figurehead at the bow is a mermaid holding a cannon in her mouth.
- Crewed by 20 sailors.

## Crew
- Jack Rivendale ("Jack of all trades") — first officer candidate; older, missing a few teeth, carries a pipe
- Claudius Taylor — quartermaster; serious demeanor; wears cracked spectacles he keeps meaning to replace; weathered coat and vest; navigates by a broken compass but has never steered anyone wrong
- Piper Barnete — the cook, also an expert juggler; wide grin, red hair, prankster energy
- Clayton Hale — expert sailor who loves the sea above all else; weathered skin, blue eyes, dark hair tied at the nape of his neck
- Oscar Hoggarde — master of arms; deaf in one ear from an explosion he set off while in the Crevalon Navy; was discharged for it

## Connections
- Used by the party for sea travel between Crevalon's coastal cities
- At one point carried 2.5 tons of Afuku trade goods (later sold in Finreld)
