# Morvir

## Type
- City (drow city / Underdark-adjacent)
- Part of: the Underdark beneath Crevalon

## Description
- Morvir is a drow city, home to followers of Lolth.
- The city contains a main square used for public executions.
- The earlier era party traveled here to rescue Sister Fiona and Javen, who were imprisoned in the city's prison.

## Key Events
- The party witnessed a public execution in the main square: a high priestess of Lolth (Avra's mother) executed a Solarian monk as an act of war, delivering a declaration that the Solarians had struck their only ally and would pay with blood.
- The priestess's name was Minva; the party fought her during their escape from the city's prison and managed to behead her.
- Sister Fiona and Javen were rescued from the prison here.

## Notable Inhabitants
- Minva — high priestess of Lolth; Avra's mother; killed by the earlier era party during the prison escape.
- Avra — a drow whose mother was Minva.

## Connections
- Underdark city; accessible via Underdark routes
- Zanzarite (Elly's home city) is nearby, described as not too far from Morvir
