# Madame Alora Venyette

## Identity
- Vampire (re-turned; originally turned by a succubus from Avernus, then made human again, then re-turned by Nikolai — a Ikovian changeling diplomat)
- Race: Human (appears as a brunette woman with pale skin and sharpened nails/fangs in vampire form)
- Also referred to as "Madame Alora" in Campaign 2
- Alias: Court mage (former title in Crevalon, before King Titus's death and the Siadiff takeover)
- Full name on record: Alora Venyette (spelled "Venette" once in the text; primary spelling throughout is Venyette)

## Role & Affiliation
- Former court mage to King Titus of Crevalon
- Connected to the dwarves of Doragon — they were among those who helped her (specifically, it was dwarves who revived her with smelling salts and provided her with animal blood after she sealed a dragon)
- In Campaign 2: protects the Ikovian queen with an arcane shield; aligns with refugees seeking refuge in Ikovia
- Centuries ago she was one of the powerful mages who participated in sealing the dragons during the DragonMage War; she used a purple gem as the arcane focus

## History & Origin
- Was originally turned into a vampire by a succubus from Avernus; she had summoned the succubus to get information on the location of a missing colleague (Arcanus Loraine, a fellow scholar lost on another plane); the succubus overtook her during the exchange
- Her husband was still alive when she was first turned; he recognized the look in her eye and defended himself; over time she tried to find a cure
- Was subsequently "staked" and her contributions to the kingdom were erased; she was awakened centuries later when the kingdom was in need
- After being awakened, she was made human again (through the efforts of a group of arcana users in Crevalon); she regretted this deeply, describing the human form as unnatural and uncomfortable
- Re-turned into a vampire by Nikolai (an Ikovian changeling diplomat) at the old manor in the Elantir Forest; she accepted his offer willingly after considering it carefully
- After being re-turned she and Nikolai developed a romantic connection; Nikolai was subsequently badly injured by werewolves in the Toven Swamps (near Siadiff); she rescued him
- Traveled to Ikovia to warn of Siadiff's corruption
- Was captured by Sir Thomas Wright (possessed by an infernal being) in Siadiff and subjected to days of radiant torture in adamantine cuffs; Nikolai (disguised as Sir Thomas) broke her out

## Abilities & Powers
- Powerful arcane mage; specializes in planar knowledge and arcane research
- Can cast invisibility, teleportation (plane-level precision), scrying, and complex arcane rituals
- Participated in sealing a dragon using a purple gem as arcane focus during the DragonMage War
- As a vampire, has enhanced senses, strength, and fangs; previously spent centuries in enchanted sleep and is not weakened by sunlight in the manner of standard vampires (details consistent with an unusual vampiric lineage, possibly due to her first turning being by a succubus from Avernus)
- Carries a sending stone

## Key Relationships
- Nikolai: Ikovian changeling diplomat who re-turned her; became her romantic partner; she rescued him from the Toven Swamps after a werewolf attack
- Florian Reiter: they share memories of the old Elantir Forest manor; she remembered their past conversation on the stairwell about his nature, and the anger she encountered from Caeda there; she remembered first attacking Altarion there as well
- Caeda: she encountered her at the manor and remembered Caeda's anger toward her
- Altarion: she remembered first attacking him at the manor
- Queen Elvira: previous queen who defended Alora but eventually sided with a dragon, which led to a falling out and a pivotal betrayal; the Queen used a force field to pin Alora to the wall
- Virion Maganti: he tampered with a shard she was connected to, causing her to be possessed
- The dwarves of Doragon: connected to her as allies who assisted her after the dragon-sealing ritual
- Lady Adrastea Orvir: possibly connected — a note mentions "Adrastea helps out her father in the magic shop" and "Madame Alora" appears at a critical scene in Campaign 2 alongside other allies including Lady Orvir

## Key Events (campaign)
- Campaign 1 (Alora.pdf): Participated in sealing a dragon using the purple gem at the summit of an icy peak; was revived by dwarves afterward; gathered arcane components in the Elantir Forest; met Nikolai while gathering those components; accepted his offer to re-turn her; was re-turned at Florian's old manor; rescued Nikolai from the Toven Swamps; went to Siadiff at the king's request to investigate wrongful imprisonment; was tortured by Sir Thomas Wright (infernally possessed); rescued by Nikolai in disguise; traveled to Ikovia together
- Campaign 2: Referred to as "Madame Alora"; appears protecting the Ikovian queen with an arcane shield; is with Nikolai; traveled through tunnels as refugees; was greeted (and briefly punched) by Adelonda upon arrival

## Important Notes
- The dwarves of Doragon are connected to Alora Venyette, NOT to Lady Orvir / Lady Adrastea Orvir; do not conflate these two characters
- Alora Venyette is NOT Lady Orvir; they are entirely separate characters
- Alora was originally turned by a succubus of Avernus (not by Florian or any paladin-linked vampire); this is a distinguishing fact
- Her previous vampiric nature left a faint trace even after she was made human (Nikolai noted "the slightest hint of your previous nature" when he fed from her during the re-turning)
- She was staked and had her contributions erased historically; she is bitter about this
- Sir Thomas Wright, who tortured her, was possessed by an infernal being (not acting of his own free will entirely, though he was also described as a "pompous bigot" before the possession)
