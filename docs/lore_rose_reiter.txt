# Rose Reiter

## Identity
- Nature/type: Player character (Campaign 2)
- Race/species: Not specified in available text
- Full name: Rose Reiter

## Role & Affiliation
- Player character controlled by "Are" (player)
- Daughter of Florian Reiter (vampire paladin, Campaign 1 protagonist) and Caeda
- Member of the Campaign 2 adventuring party recruited by Milly Varna
- Campaign 2

## History & Origin
- Born to Florian Reiter and Caeda; she is the next generation of the Reiter family introduced in Campaign 1
- Campaign 2 takes place 30 years after Campaign 1; Rose represents the Reiter lineage in the new era
- When the campaign opens, Caeda was captured by the Paladins of Siadiff while helping refugees escape through a route connecting Crevalon to Ikovia
- Florian was not with Caeda at the time of her capture and subsequently contacted Milly to retrieve Rose

## Abilities & Powers
- Not specified in DM notes beyond her role as a player character
- Received a Rope of Climbing from Milly as her starting magic item

## Key Relationships
- Florian Reiter: her father (Campaign 1 major character); worked alongside Rose to search for Caeda; eventually decided the search was too dangerous for Rose and brought her back to Milly's manor
- Caeda: her mother; captured by Paladins of Siadiff during Campaign 2
- Milly Varna: sheltered Rose at her manor; was the one to inform Rose of Caeda's capture and urge her not to attempt an immediate rescue
- The Campaign 2 party (Valentin Mirakov, Wren of the Standing Stars, Lezlie Mackerel, Adelona Arvind): fellow party members
- Adelona Arvind: confessed to Rose that she was a dragon (a significant reveal in the campaign)

## Key Events (Campaign 2)
- One of the five original player characters imprisoned by paladins at the start of Campaign 2, later rescued by Milly
- Was present when Milly delivered the news that Caeda had been captured; Rose's quiet, soft-spoken reaction was noted
- Milly placed her hand on Rose's shoulder and teleported with her back to the manor while the rest of the party continued the staff mission
- Rose went with Florian to search for Caeda; they fought through a guarded underground cave network near the mountain ridge border
- When Caeda could not be found at that location, Florian determined it was too dangerous to continue with Rose and returned her to Milly's manor
- Reunited with the party at the manor later in the campaign; Val and Adelona filled her in on recent events
- Yuri was absent from the manor during Rose's return visit

## Important Notes
- Rose is listed as a player character on D&D Beyond under the Campaign 2 campaign link
- Her last name Reiter connects her directly to Florian Reiter of Campaign 1
- Do not confuse Rose Reiter (Florian and Caeda's daughter, Campaign 2 PC) with any general use of the word "rose" in the text
- Campaign 2 takes place 30 years after Campaign 1; Rose is a new-generation character, not a returning PC
