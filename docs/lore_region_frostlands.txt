# The FrostLands

## Type
- Region (arctic/glacial region)
- Part of: Crevalon (far north, beyond the northern forest)

## Description
- The FrostLands is a bleak, frozen arctic region in the far north of Crevalon (and potentially extending beyond the kingdom's borders).
- Created during the DragonMage War when the white dragon blew its icy breath across the north, forming the FrostLands permanently.
- The landscape is described as a "bleak landscape" of heaps of snow, glaciers, and frozen terrain; the wind whips relentlessly; the cold is severe.
- North of the Elantir Forest, beyond a "bigger icy mountain range," lie glaciers.
- The region was where Alora Venette performed the ancient dragon-sealing ritual: she climbed to a summit in the FrostLands, coordinated with Valerion to the West, and cast the binding orb spell to seal the dragon there.
- A retired wizard (formerly of Diondria) became a recluse in the FrostLands; he was later killed by Virion Maganti, who took over his tower. The tower is referenced as "the wizard's tower in the north" and is a location Virion uses as one of his bases.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- A wizard's tower in the FrostLands — originally belonging to a powerful retired mage from Diondria; rumored to contain a cubic gate (a magic item allowing planar travel); later seized by Virion Maganti as one of his bases; called "the winter tower" in some previously notes; used by the Bluejay (an Aviary leader) as a secured location
- Glaciers in the far north — the ancient battlefield of the dragon-sealing ritual; where one of the dragons was sealed

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- Virion Maganti — occupies the wizard's tower in the FrostLands as one of his multiple bases
- The Bluejay (an Aviary leader) — recently secured the winter tower for herself and Virion as an Aviary base
- Erius Orvir — father of Lady Adrastea Orvir; became a recluse and is possibly based in the FrostLands after his wife Gwentharidel went missing on another plane; Adrastea suggests he "became a recluse after what happened to mother"

## History & Lore
- Origin: During the DragonMage War thousands of years ago, the white dragon serving the Lunarian Queen blew icy breath across the north, permanently creating the FrostLands.
- The sealing ritual: Alora Venette (one of the most powerful mages of her era) performed a dragon-sealing ritual at the summit of the FrostLands. She carried a purple gem charged with a human soul. Valerion provided a western signal (shower of orange sparks). Alora expanded the orb of arcane energy until it sealed the dragon. The gem returned to her hand and the dragon was imprisoned. This feat exhausted Alora completely and led to her being found by dwarves who revived her.
- A second sealing ("the Celestials were sealed; the enchantment worked") also occurred, suggesting multiple powerful beings were bound.

## Connections
- Far north of the Elantir Forest, beyond the mountain range visible from Diondria
- Alora Venette's story begins and historically connects to the FrostLands
- Virion uses the tower here as a staging base for his schemes
- Lady Adrastea Orvir's father Erius may be in this region as a recluse

## Important Notes
- The FrostLands were created by the WHITE DRAGON during the DragonMage War — NOT by magic of the Lunarians or Solarians directly.
- The SLAERIAN DESERT was created by the RED DRAGON — these are two distinct geographic results of two different dragons in the same war.
- The wizard's tower in the FrostLands is NOT Taelreth (which is in Diondria) and NOT the Hailthorne Institute (which is in Anearios). It is a separate, private wizard's tower.
- Virion Maganti occupies this tower as a villain base — it is NOT a safe or neutral location by previously.
