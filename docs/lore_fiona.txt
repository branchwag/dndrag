# Sister Fiona

## Identity
- Nature/type: Elven cleric / healer / religious traveler
- Race/species: Elf (female)
- Known as: Sister Fiona; later referred to simply as Fiona
- No surname given in available text

## Role & Affiliation
- Member (and designated traveling emissary) of the Manasseh Tribe, which resides in a secluded temple deep within the Autumngrove Woods and worships the god Rao
- Serves at the Temple of Rao in Finreld while on her world-traveling mission
- Officiated the triple wedding at the end of the earlier era (marrying Khadja + the Dust Man, Florian + Caeda, Alora + Altarion, Elly + Scirocco)
- the earlier era

## History & Origin
- Member of the Manasseh Tribe, an isolated elven community in the Autumngrove Woods
- The tribe is led by a female elder over 800 years old who handpicks one male and one female elf to serve as "childbearers" for the tribe for a generation; when a new pair is selected, previous childbearers' descendants (Fiona's many brothers and sisters) make up the tribe population
- All tribe members not designated as childbearers take a vow of celibacy
- Every 10 years, the tribe holds a ritual to determine the most "enlightened" member (excluding childbearers and elder); that person is sent to travel the world and return knowledge to the tribe
- Fiona was nominated as this traveling emissary "fairly recently" and had just begun her quest at the time of the earlier era
- Friends with Ari (the rouge player character): met through the Temple of Rao in Finreld; she helped reform him after "a murder gone wrong"

## Abilities & Powers
- Cleric of Rao: healing spells (Cure Wounds, Revivify), Remove Curse, Dispel Evil and Good, Spare the Dying
- Protective magic: conjured a shimmering blue force field over a group of children during a creature attack in the Finreld marketplace
- Anti-magic shackles prevent her spellcasting when cuffed (she was unable to heal herself while captured)
- Cannot heal without a diamond for Revivify; the cost was a point of negotiation in one session

## Key Relationships
- Ari: fellow Finreld resident; he instigated her rescue from the Underdark; they are close friends; he is associated with the Temple of Rao
- Captain Karim: treated Karim's near-fatal necrotic wound (Remove Curse followed by healing); moved Karim into her own room during recovery; the two grew close
- Javen: a drow male who fell in love with her; they were both captured by the drow and tortured; Fiona admitted she loved him but said the circumstances made it impossible ("I do love him — it's just the circumstances we find ourselves in are unfortunate"); she is celibate by her tribe's rules
- The the earlier era party: healed multiple party members; officiated the end-of-campaign weddings
- Commodore Julius Webb: present at the Temple of Rao when he visited Karim; interacted with Fiona in that context

## Key Events (the earlier era)
- First appearance at the Temple of Rao in Finreld: rushed to aid Captain Karim when the party carried her in near death; another healer scoffed at treating a tiefling and Fiona silenced this with "All deserve Rao's mercy"; cast Remove Curse to lift the necrotic wound, then healed Karim
- Seen outside the Temple leading a field trip of elven children in an arithmetic lesson in the Finreld marketplace; described as tall with long red hair and sparkling green eyes, wearing religious order robes
- Abducted: during the marketplace lesson, a pack of quaggoth-like creatures hauled Fiona away over the city wall; the children reported her taken
- Rescued from the Underdark (drow city of Morvir): the party found her in the prison, heavily lacerated and kneeling in a pool of blood, bound with adamantine anti-magic shackles; she begged the party to also rescue Javen
- Participated in the escape from Morvir during the drow resistance rebellion
- End of the earlier era: officiated the triple (quadruple) wedding ceremony; her line: "I have to say that this is the most people I have ever wed at the same time" (said nervously); the ceremony was conducted in Rao's name using the standard marriage rite

## Important Notes
- Appearance: tall elven woman; long red hair; sparkling green eyes (in marketplace description); in other scenes she has "kind hazel eyes and quite pretty features" (the two descriptions may reflect different lighting/scenes)
- Voice: "soft gentle yet firm, British" — the wedding ceremony specifically notes "British female soft voice"
- She is a Manasseh tribe member — she is not a standard Temple of Rao nun, although she wears the robes; her religious role is as a traveling emissary, not a lifelong resident nun
- Her vow of celibacy is a tribe requirement, not a Temple of Rao requirement; this made her relationship with Javen complicated
- Do not conflate her with other Temple of Rao figures; Sister Valrieth is a separate, less sympathetic healer at the same temple (the one who scoffed at treating a tiefling)
- The Autumngrove Woods (her tribe's home) is noted as potentially being near the Dove / Aviary's base of operations, though this connection is speculative in the DM notes
