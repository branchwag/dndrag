# Ari Crownsilver

## Identity
- Wizard / Rogue multiclass; subclass: Bladesinger (took Bladesinger as his subclass during Campaign 1)
- Race: High Elf
- Full name: Andresti "Ari" Crownsilver
- Codename during Aviary years: The Raven
- Player character in Campaign 1

## Role & Affiliation
- Student and associated member of the Sylvanian Academy of Magical Arts in Handletare; admitted by letter of recommendation from Florian Reiter during Campaign 1
- Former member of The Aviary (an underground organization; resigned two years before Campaign 1 begins)
- Informal agent of King Titus alongside the rest of the Campaign 1 party
- Originally from Rellond
- Lived in Finreld for two years after leaving The Aviary, renting the attic of a townhouse hosting The Blue Harp Inn; gnome innkeeper Russell Buttonbottom calls him "Mr. Crownsilver"

## History & Origin
- Grew up in an orphanage; no one came to take him from it; this abandonment is a defining wound ("No one cared about you. No one came to take you away from that orphanage")
- Was a member of The Aviary as The Raven; left after "a murder gone wrong"
- Was befriended by Fiona (a priestess at the Temple of Rao in Finreld) who helped reform him after that incident
- His parents were immigrants from Anearios who came to Crevalon to practice magic (magic is banned in Anearios as open spellcasting); this is why there was no family to raise him in Crevalon
- His grandfather is still alive in Anearios and is an inventor; Campaign 3 DM notes suggest this grandfather could appear

## Abilities & Powers
- Bladesinger: combines arcane magic with martial combat; can use Bladesong feature; fights with blades alongside spellcasting
- Rogue abilities: stealth, Dust of Disappearance (starting magic item), thieving skills; was trained by The Bluejay (a changeling Aviary member) in combat and rogue arts
- Arcane abilities: growing magical repertoire over Campaign 1; admitted to the Sylvanian Academy's curriculum to formally study wizardry
- Owns an owl familiar named Rao (after the god)

## Key Relationships
- Lady Adrastea Orvir: deeply in love with her; their relationship developed over Campaign 1 — meetings at the Academy, a confectionary "date," dancing at Lady Vesemir's masquerade; she shows genuine warmth toward him; she stopped time to protect him at the masquerade; Florian asked Ari to "check in on Lady Orvir" for him
- Florian Reiter: mentor and benefactor; Florian recommended him to the Sylvanian Academy, sparred with him, gave him combat advice, provided him with an Oathbow (via a letter to Pavel), and offered him guidance on mixing with aristocracy
- The Bluejay: former trainer in The Aviary; Ari still has feelings for her and tried to reach her; she gave him information about The Dove being an immortal Fey being and about where Virion "hid the keys"
- The party (Elly, Scirocco, Babushka, Caeda, Pavel): Campaign 1 companions
- King Titus: received royal title and a castle from the King as reward for Campaign 1 service

## Key Events (campaign)
- Campaign 1: Joined the party in Finreld; disclosed to Florian that he had been a member of The Aviary and wanted to take them down; Florian stressed going after the head of the organization, The Dove
- Attempted to pry open the puzzle box at the bookshop; suffered greatly — horrible whispered voices and nearly lost consciousness; the curse was later removed by Elly
- Purchased magical items in Adelanto (a raven, a bluejay, and a blue camel in glasswork)
- Trained with Florian in the Elantir Forest; took Bladesinger as his subclass
- Attended Lady Vesemir's masquerade ball with Lady Orvir as his companion (they danced)
- Had a midnight meeting with the Bluejay after the white dragon fight; she let slip that The Dove was an immortal Fey being
- Was awarded a royal title and a castle by King Titus

## Important Notes
- He was a former member of The Aviary under the codename The Raven — NOT currently affiliated with them
- He is IN LOVE with Lady Orvir; this is established clearly across multiple sessions
- His family's Anearios connection (parents were immigrants fleeing the magic ban) is a potential plot hook for Campaign 3
- "He was a Crevalon viscount and a defender of the Crown. He was a Bladesinger" — this is how he identifies himself later in Campaign 1
- He kept his Aviary past hidden initially and disclosed it only to Florian in private
