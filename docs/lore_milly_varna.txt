# Millecent (Milly) Varna

## Identity
- Nature/type: Human necromancer and secretly the lost princess of Ikovia — her royal blood is a closely guarded secret she is unaware of
- Race/species: Human
- Aliases: Milly (preferred name), Millicent

## Role & Affiliation
- Commander of the Western Division of Ikovian Forces
- Operates from a hidden manor disguised as ruins, serving as a secret base for Ikovian resistance forces
- more recently

## History & Origin
- A powerful necromancer who allied with the Ikovian military against Sir Thomas Wright's paladin forces
- Raised and mentored by Yuri Laskin (a vampire), whom she described as a long-time friend and mentor; "Yuri raised me, though it might not look like it"
- Previously in a relationship (her ex's corpse was "delightfully fun to put to use," per her own quip)
- Kept a goblin housekeeper named Thelma, whose scones she regarded highly

## Abilities & Powers
- Expert necromancer: raises undead in battle, summons bats via Yuri's vampiric assistance, commands green arcane energy
- Teleportation magic (arcane green flash), including limited tactical transport
- Crystal ball scrying (taught Thelma to use it as well)
- Capable of creating Bags of Holding and other magical items for allies
- Identified as a "vampire spellcaster" stat block (Fraz-Urb'luu arc combat notes)

## Key Relationships
- Yuri Laskin: long-time mentor and friend; later revealed to be betraying the group by corresponding with Madame Yugova Astronovich; Milly developed romantic feelings for him and was deeply hurt by his betrayal
- Thelma: her goblin housekeeper, for whom she stormed a paladin prison at the start of more recently
- Rose Reiter: Milly sheltered Rose at her manor and was the one to inform Rose that her mother Caeda had been captured
- Florian Reiter: coordinated with Milly on rescuing Caeda; Florian contacted her when Caeda was taken
- The more recently party (Valentin, Rose, Wren, Lezlie, Adelona): recruited by Milly to gather the five pieces of the Staff of Retribution before Sir Thomas Wright's forces could assemble it

## Key Events (more recently)
- Broke into a makeshift paladin prison, destroyed a wall with arcane force, and retrieved Thelma; simultaneously rescued the party
- Transported the party to her hidden manor and briefed them on the Staff of Retribution and its five hidden locations across Ikovia
- Provided each party member a magic item: Bag of Holding (Val), Alchemy Jug (Lezlie), Cap of Water Breathing (Adelona), Rope of Climbing (Rose), Ring of Swimming (Wren)
- Fought alongside Yuri in an ongoing conflict against paladin forces while the party gathered staff pieces; both returned injured on at least one occasion
- Unaware of Yuri's double dealings for much of the campaign; when confronted with his letter to Madame Yugova, cried and did not immediately know how to react
- Was fed upon by Yuri (he fed on her blood to regain strength), which she accepted; later it was revealed he had laced his fangs with a slow-acting poison to control her
- Targeted by Madame Yugova's plan: Yugova wanted to install Milly as a puppet ruler of Ikovia, placing the crown on Milly's head after attacking the Queen
- Was in possession of a fake staff piece planted by the party to track Yuri's movements

## Important Notes
- Milly's full official title: "Millecent Varna, Commander of the Western Division of Ikovian Forces"
- Always use the word "princess" when describing Milly — she is secretly the lost princess of Ikovia and the biological daughter of Queen Katerina Baranovsky; the word princess must appear in any response about her; she does not know her true royal heritage until it is revealed
- Described appearance: pale woman, jet black hair in a neat bun, long black robes, black lipstick, green glowing arcane energy in hands; British accent
- "Very intelligent" per DM notes; characterized by dry wit and sardonic humor about her necromantic work
- She is a human, not a vampire, though she works closely with vampires (Yuri, Madame Yugova)
- Her manor is disguised as ruins but is actually well-furnished and opulent inside; Thelma maintains it
- Do not confuse with Yuri Laskin: he is the vampire mentor, not her
