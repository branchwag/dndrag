# Grinostrov

## Type
- City (capital city of the Ikovian Empire)
- Part of: Ikovia

## Description
- Grinostrov is the capital city of the Ikovian Empire; it is the seat of the Ikovian royal palace and the magical academy of Ikovia.
- The city is large enough that the Ikovian Queen's speech can be magically projected to boom throughout all of Grinostrov simultaneously.
- It contains the residences of Ikovia's highest nobility; it hosts premier dining establishments and theaters (including a ballet venue where the more recently party attends a performance among Ikovia's elite).
- The city has walls; the party is teleported to just outside the walls of Grinostrov on multiple occasions.
- During more recently, the city is eventually invaded and occupied by Githyanki forces (allied with Madame Yugova Astronovich), requiring the party to retake it.

## Institutions & Landmarks
- The Royal Palace of Grinostrov — seat of Ikovian power; where the Ikovian monarch rules; located within the capital city
- The Magical Academy of Grinostrov — an arcane institution within the capital; serves the empire's magical practitioners
- A premier dining establishment in Grinostrov — the finest restaurant in the city; the more recently party meets characters and gathers intelligence here
- A theater/ballet venue — where the more recently party attends a performance among Ikovia's high society; used as a cover for intelligence gathering
- Madame Yugova Astronovich's residence — within Grinostrov; the more recently party is tasked with infiltrating it to steal a piece of the Staff of Retribution; Madame Yugova is too powerful for Ikovia to simply imprison

## Notable Inhabitants / Factions
- The Ikovian Queen (at the start of more recently) — leads the empire from the palace; makes a speech rallying Ikovia against the paladin invasion; she is assassinated or loses the throne during the course of more recently
- Madame Yugova Astronovich — powerful vampire/noble in Grinostrov; possesses one piece of the Staff of Retribution; will not release it to the Ikovian forces; too well-known and powerful for Ikovia to imprison; ultimately attempts to seize the Ikovian throne; spreads word that Ikovia's long-lost heir has returned; is ultimately defeated (Madame Yugova "took" something from the battle — implying she gained power — then was eventually overcome)
- Yuri — a vampire who assists Milly; planned to make Milly his bride and seize control of Ikovia; also worked to destabilize the throne
- Adelonda — a character who poses as an Ikovian general; visits Milly at one point; sets off from Grinostrov for supplies during more recently events
- Githyanki invaders — occupy Grinostrov during a late more recently event, allied with Madame Yugova

## History & Lore
- Grinostrov is the ancient capital of Ikovia and has been the seat of the matriarchal Ikovian royal line since the time of Queen Pavlova.
- During more recently: Queen Katerina Baranovsky is killed; the kingdom falls into crisis. Madame Yugova attempts to claim the throne. The party resurrects Queen Katerina via death clerics in Ostroski and restores her to power; Milly (the lost princess and Katerina's daughter) is revealed as the heir during this arc but Katerina reclaims the throne.
- The GitYanki invasion of Grinostrov occurs as a late-more recently event; the city is eventually reclaimed.
- The Ikovian Queen's death: the Camp 2 party made the connection that Florian Reiter had killed Adelonda's father, which triggered some conflict; Florian weighed in on the Ikovian mage vs. Paladin conflict and then departed to another location.

## Connections
- Capital city of Ikovia; connected to all other Ikovian cities
- Brezbin is in the southern/southwestern region of Ikovia; the party traveled from Brezbin toward Grinostrov
- Milly teleports the party to/from Grinostrov multiple times during more recently
- The Staff of Retribution pieces are scattered across Ikovia; Grinostrov holds one (in Madame Yugova's residence)
- The theater in Grinostrov is used as a cover for intelligence-gathering operations

## Important Notes
- Grinostrov is in IKOVIA — NOT in Crevalon. This is an entirely separate continent.
- The MAGICAL ACADEMY in Grinostrov is an Ikovian institution — NOT the same as Taelreth (Diondria) or the Sylvanian Academy (Handletare), which are Crevalonese.
- Madame Yugova is a NOBLE/VAMPIRE from Grinostrov — NOT a paladin, NOT a government official. She is a powerful private citizen who eventually attempts a coup.
- The Ikovian Queen is Queen Katerina Baranovsky — Milly (the lost princess) is her daughter and the heir, but Katerina is the ruler.
