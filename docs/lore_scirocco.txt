# Scirocco

## Identity
- Air genasi (blue-skinned genasi from the desert; described as "a blue-skinned genasi druid from the desert, harnessing the power of air and lightning")
- Race: Air genasi (half-human, half-djinn)
- Player character in the earlier era
- In a romantic relationship with Ellyjobell (Elly) during the earlier era

## Role & Affiliation
- Druid of the Crevalon desert region (Slaerian Desert / Adelanto area)
- Informal agent of King Titus alongside the rest of the the earlier era party
- From the desert settlement of Adelanto; raised there by the community and a shaman

## History & Origin
- Her mother is Khadja, a djinn from the Plane of Air (specifically from the City of Avis on the Plane of Air)
- Her father is Virion Maganti: he used a Censer of Controlling Air Elementals to summon Khadja, forced her to give up the child, and then imprisoned Khadja within a ring of djinni summoning
- After being handed over, Scirocco was given to a desert shaman who blessed her with Druidic powers
- Was raised in Adelanto; has a non-biological brother named Malek (sarcastic and charming; loves hippogriff racing)
- The community leader of Adelanto is Samir; Scirocco grew up knowing her
- Her grandmother: Khadja's mother (a djinn); Scirocco's grandmother is referenced in the earlier era as "Duchess Jamila" (the party visited her home on the Plane of Air later in the campaign)

## Abilities & Powers
- Druid abilities: Wild Shape (including shark form; used this in an underwater encounter), Nature spells, conjuring food and water
- Air genasi traits: connection to the Plane of Air; can hold her breath underwater naturally (did not need underwater breathing potions unlike others)
- Inherited some connection to the Plane of Air through her mother Khadja
- Khadja noted she could theoretically help free her from the ring if a portal or great mage could be found (Khadja cannot transport herself from the ring)

## Key Relationships
- Ellyjobell (Elly): her romantic partner during the earlier era; Florian was attracted to Scirocco but accepted she was taken
- Khadja: her mother; imprisoned in a ring of djinni summoning by Virion; Scirocco discovered her existence when the party found the ring in Virion's tomb
- Virion Maganti: her biological father; he does not acknowledge the relationship; he imprisoned Khadja to prevent her from being a weakness to him
- Malek: her non-biological brother in Adelanto; they have the standard sibling banter; he loves hippogriff racing
- Samir: the community leader of Adelanto who raised her alongside the community
- Florian Reiter: he was attracted to her; expressed this via a sending stone ("Scirocco — I know what I am but why must you injure me so?"); Scirocco gave him the stone back or used it; he accepted gracefully
- "Grandmother": referred to as Duchess Jamila; the party visited her on the Plane of Air

## Key Events (campaign)
- the earlier era: Present from the start in Sharnwick; touched the Solarian talisman and resisted its mental pull; traveled throughout Crevalon and the desert with the party
- Discovered her mother Khadja imprisoned in a ring in Virion's tomb; had a heart-to-heart with her mother after the hippogriff races in Adelanto
- Was informed by Khadja that Virion is her father
- Received a sending stone from Florian after a moonlit flight together (Florian had asked her on the flight after confirming she was with Elly; he was crestfallen but still gave her the stone)
- Traveled to the Plane of Air to visit her grandmother Duchess Jamila
- Won the hippogriff race in Adelanto (earning Ari and Babushka significant gold from their bets)

## Important Notes
- Scirocco's connection to the Plane of Air comes from her mother Khadja (a djinn), not from any independent training
- Her father Virion Maganti is the main villain of the earlier era; she did not know this at the start
- Her grandmother is Khadja's mother, a djinn — referred to as "Duchess Jamila" in the text
- The DM notes reference "Scirocco's theme song — I'm blue" as flavor
- She is bisexual / queer — in a relationship with Elly (a female gnome)
