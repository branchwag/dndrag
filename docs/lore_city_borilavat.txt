# Borilavat

## Type
- Settlement (northern cold-weather town)
- Part of: Crevalon's northern reaches, near the Frostlands

## Description
- Borilavat is a cold, remote settlement further north than most Crevalon cities, situated near the Frostlands.
- The settlement's structures are tent-like in construction but remarkably sturdy — mammoth tusks form the frames, with various hides wrapped around them to make the exterior walls.
- Gnomes and humans make up the population; residents can be seen cooking outdoors and crafting wares.
- Yeti are known to inhabit the surrounding area.
- Wulfgar (a Frostlands barbarian) hailed from a tribe slightly north of Borilavat.

## Notable Inhabitants
- Vilolski — a resident the earlier era party met here; the party saved the village from a goblin attack.
- The Babushka — an NPC connected to this settlement.

## Key Events
- The earlier era party traveled here and defended the settlement against goblin raiders.

## Connections
- Located near the southern edge of the Frostlands
- One of the northernmost inhabited settlements in Crevalon's sphere
