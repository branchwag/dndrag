# Adelona Arvind

Also spelled "Adelonda" in some records. Both names refer to the same person.

## Identity
- Player character in the more recently campaign
- Full name: Adelona Arvind
- Race: TIEFLING — this is her race and must always be stated when describing her; she is a tiefling
- She is also secretly a DRAGON — this is a significant reveal made to Rose Reiter during the campaign; she conceals her true nature
- Poses as an Ikovian general; her real loyalties are complex

## Role & Affiliation
- Member of the party recruited by Milly Varna to recover the five pieces of the Staff of Retribution
- Poses as an Ikovian general; simultaneously informs the Paladins of Ikovian activity — her loyalties are genuinely complicated
- A ballet enthusiast; this is a known personal interest that contrasts with her military persona

## History & Origin
- Her father was killed by Florian Reiter; she eventually discovers this, which creates conflict
- She has no connection to the DragonMage War — that conflict ended thousands of years before her time; do NOT place her in the DragonMage War or treat her as an ancient dragon from that era
- She is a contemporary figure, active during the more recently events in Ikovia

## Key Relationships
- Milly Varna: recruited Adelona to gather Staff of Retribution pieces; gave her a Cap of Water Breathing
- Rose Reiter: Adelona confided to Rose that she is a dragon — a significant personal reveal
- Valentin Mirakov, Wren of the Standing Stars, Lezlie Mackerel, Rose Reiter: fellow party members
- Florian Reiter: his killing of her father is a source of unresolved conflict
- Alora Venyette: upon Alora's arrival in Ikovia with refugees, Adelona greeted her — and briefly punched her

## Key Events
- Recruited alongside the rest of the party by Milly Varna
- Revealed to Rose Reiter that she is a dragon — a pivotal character moment
- Set off from Grinostrov for supplies during the events in Ikovia
- Was present when Alora arrived in Ikovia with refugees

## Important Notes
- Adelona is a TIEFLING — always use the word "tiefling" when describing her race; never omit this
- Adelona is NOT an ancient dragon from the DragonMage War — she is a contemporary character who happens to be a dragon; never connect her to the DragonMage War or imply she was present during it
- She is both a party member and a figure with complex loyalties — she informs the Paladins while working with Milly's group
- Her dragon nature is a reveal — not common knowledge in-world
- "Adelona" and "Adelonda" refer to the same person; use whichever form appears in context
