# The Plane of Air

## Type
- Plane (Elemental Plane of Air)

## Description
- The Plane of Air is an open, vast expanse of sky; those who arrive find themselves standing on a small chunk of earth suspended in this endless sky, with a gentle whoosh of wind ruffling hair and clothes.
- In the distance, clouds heavy with rain flash with lightning.
- At the heart of the plane, elaborate white structures rise from drifting clouds — a city built from condensation itself.
- Within the city, winding paths snake between buildings toward a central area, though most inhabitants hover or fly rather than walk.
- The population includes celestial-adjacent figures and Aaracokra — bird-like people with elegant brown and white feathered wings.
- The rushing winds carry a great energy throughout the city; the overall atmosphere is one of boundless movement and light.

## Connections
- Accessed via teleportation or planar travel
- The earlier era party traveled here during their investigation into celestial and dragon-related conflicts
